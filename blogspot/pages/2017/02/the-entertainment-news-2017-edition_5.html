ACCA 13th District: I like how he goes to some places and he's thought of being overly enthusiastic and on some places he's thought of being complacent. Ah, it's hard being Jean.
This new episode (4) talks about another of the 13 districts of Dowa, the most secluded of them, the most independent of them, the most lagging behind - Suitsu District. It talks mostly about how backwards in technology and life it is with the others after it gained independence off the royal rule, and thus has never been improving, ie. it has been stuck as it is a century ago. With it comes a whole level of political rule (led by royals wanting to preserve the place while commoners cannot do much but live life), a different level of rebellion (even the ACCA officers and royal militants want to help change the place up) and a whole different level of empathy. I mean, look at it. As much as it looks great as an antique vase, it's still antique. And nothing old and alive will remain vibrant. Nothing wrong about closing one eye about it, but somehow Jean got into a coup for no reason. And hey, if it's a topic of conversation, it will be.
This show is great. I like how it expresses the nation well while still focusing on the main story. I mean, Jean's no bad guy. He doesn't even look like one. Look at him. He only cares for smoke haha
