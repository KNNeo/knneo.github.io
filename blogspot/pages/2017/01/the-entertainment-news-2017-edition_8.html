ANIME
Sangatsu no Lion: Damn this episode (12) is starting out strong with YUKI singing the opening theme song, I must say. Okay, theme songs aside first, the story itself. Kiriyama recovered from his flu from like, two weeks ago (it let me wait that long, okay?) and now we move on to his shougi matches. We have quite a clear path to what is happening, and that Kiriyama's fate may be to face of with someone he has, I would say, a physical woe, in place. Don't ask me what he did to Kyouka to deserve him getting beaten considering Kyouka loves a married man and Kiriyama just got dragged in to this somehow I guess, but still. We'll definitely hear of that b*tch again in future episodes, so that's something to (not) look forward to. Other than that, I can see why Kiriyama must ditch the warm live of the Kawamoto sisters, but then again he kind of has a reason. Remember the last time he was accepted into a family and he ruined it? Yeah. That's what I was thinking when Momo wanted him to stay over at night.
