(I've heard at least two more shows who use that alphabet) which can do mind control, and just like BLEACH if you have been reading this whole post faithfully for like three years lol, you would know it's tough to manage (controlled zombies, I mean), and in this case now that makes Atsuhi be a victim to his own past and powers. Sangatsu no Lion: Okay, this kind of builds on the fact that you can have retarded friends (according to Kiriyama) like Nikaidou, and I'll explain why. First, the lead up. Remember that close to romantic comedy we received last episode? It's back, but it's not the girl. Takahashi, that baseball player someone is really into, turns out to know of people who love shougi, probably including himself. Kiriyama noticed they can hit well (not in baseball unfortunately haha) after a conversation or two, but then Takahashi showed him that clip. Starring the professional shougi player on TV with Nikaidou doing commentary. *sigh* I totally feel you Kiriyama. Just like some other Anime who has a silent main guy (*wink* Hyouka *wink*) it's tough living with an assertive friend and people being envious about your situation which is bad in your opinion. Nonetheless, it triggers him somehow, and now he has to teach people Japanese chess. Hey, I'm not saying it's bad, but at some point I see myself already leaving when Nikaidou comes in every time. Come on, who wants to have direct conflict? Well, not unless you're playing a shougi match of course. And no, I'm not interested in the game, so no thanks for the explanations, Bodoro. *laughs*

And that, even so, is still not working on this dorkhead. Seriously, Miyakoshi-san, you need classes in being human. Kisaki is ordering that foreigner like a slave, Nagata-san is dejected as usual because she also cannot express her feelings for the main guy, and don't ask me why someone doesn't want to go to school but wants to go down South. To Okinawa, for example. Damn, this show is weird. Just like Shiho-san with words on poor people lol

I'll hold my comments for now until I have the time to listen to them two weeks from now, but until then at least I'll be damned if WORKING!! theme songs will come up next week, after I'm done with my first wave of exams.
