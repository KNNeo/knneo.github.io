Is it really a problem to know too much people in your life?

To me, maybe. But have you ever wondered if everyone who knows you will remember you and be in contact with you? I'm no socialite, so this definitely is a no-no for me. Because what is important is those treasured, from friends to family to workmates and so on. And having too many of them without knowing whether you have or will tie up the knots or leave them dangling... I worry more than others, in my case.
