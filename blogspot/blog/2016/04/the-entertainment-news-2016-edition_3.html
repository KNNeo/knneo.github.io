ANIME
Assassination Classroom S2: Eh... flashback? I mean, the second half really isn't important. Apparently just random stuff. I thought it'd signal the school system or something, but I guess not. Asano Gakushu (let's refer to his name from now on) that Principal apparently had a similar past as Koro-sensei, despite being less of a scale and Gakushu being less of a octopus than that freak. Nonetheless, it was always without harm, Koro-sensei, but  for Gakushu, he has turned evil. Easily explained by the fact that as a tutor, he appreciates his own efforts and wants to see results, really kind of opposite of what a teacher should expect teaching students. Furthermore, everyone can be a part of a person's education, so sorry, Gakushu, you aren't the most important person after all. Koro-sensei, on the other hand, lives his past, so I guess for now he's the winner. Will that octopus be defeated? We can but hope not. Because for a simple reason, perhaps. Koro-sensei has never wanted to eliminate Earth after all. Man, if this was the spoilers from the Manga then I'm sorry. *laughs*
Dagashi Kashi (last episode): Yeah, for a last episode this is most definitely unsatisfactory. Seriously. But then again, this show doesn't hit much on serious topics, so I won't say much. I mean, they even has to turn Hotaru-san lesbian in order to talk about love, I already know this show is kind of a screw up already. But then thinking along the sides for the author and animation production, I guess this is good enough. Didn't expect that Sakuma Drops to debut at the end of this Anime series, because I just happen to have one. Well, technically I have two now, after recently discovering that Fue Ranume also is available at my local convenience store.
Thanks Japanese sweets; you have made my life much sweeter (and my teeth much worse) with these products. Really, if your life till even after you finished watching this show didn't consist of Sakuma Drops, Hi-Chew, Fur Ramune or even that super concentrated (8,2) milk candy... SUCKS TO BE YOU, SUCKER!! *laughs hard* *sucks a sweet*
