So, if you need any guesses, we might see a lot more about the family of Gotoh in future episodes maybe that Hime-chan may still not know of. Like, for example, her grandparents. Moving on!! Well, I'm not going to talk about how nice a world of kindness would be, but kind of like Kakushi I'm glad to be able to get a job that I would prefer that is NOT my university major which is still willing to hire me all these couple of years without trying to sh*t on me and still gave me a pay raise. And of course in all coincidences, it's also the day he is bringing out Hime-chan to play in a theme park. Well, it's one of those kid experience theme parks so the parents don't get to join, but hey, this makes this a lot more convenient. But that kind of also means he didn't get to watch Hime do all the job cosplay (lol she be doing it better than the father haha kawaii) which I hope there is recorded footage for him to keep (maybe), but that also means he can deal with the signing in which its location is just within the same mall building. I like how the author then touches on the "I don't believe I'm that famous for anyone to come" type of character but obviously, considering his manga sells like hotcakes, there are of course, hired genuine fans who come to get his signed autograph. I guess I'll never get the purpose of fanatics behind all this just because, but suffice it to say he was able to juggle both of them well. Almost got found out, but you know, Hime-chan doesn't recognise the father in his manga author cosplay haha. In the end, Hime-chan liked the bookstore job the most (no seriously Hime, go watch Denki-gai or Honda-san and tell me again) (although I would argue a "Maru Maru no Hime-chan" would be way awesome of a sequel lol), and the father got away with it once again. Hey, for someone whose objective is to totally not allow the daughter to know his job isn't... a stable job, for starters, this is as much as he can avoid it being a total disaster haha. And... reverse flashback!! I swear if you didn't even weep at that last part you are not human. *laughs* Today Hime-chan found the new boxes dated her current age probably. Not like we know of what her world is now (for example, does she even know of manga, and is she a fan?), but I guess considering she can understand the manga itself makes you wonder at what point they will reveal the father's job thing, or if the father is even around anymore. Hey, in case you were wondering, now is a good time to think about it. Cya guys next week.
