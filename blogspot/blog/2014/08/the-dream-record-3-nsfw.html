THE FANSERVICE
Hmm how shall I put this...

I was going to the public showers (like in a swimming pool) with a bunch of people in a camp* (and yes it probably includes females) and surprisingly we all went into the "female toilet" - actually its a toilet with majority female cubicles. But that doesn't mean there isn't for males; it's just a little smaller. In fact so much smaller the low ceiling height (a little above me, and I'm about 1.7m) and that we're squeezing and fighting for the cubicles after we settled down. (Be reminded that I'm a male here) I... 'assisted' one of the girls to find an empty cubicle (did I mention the male area is so much smaller: I did a comparison) and then missed a few chances to get my own. Some even resorted to using the female cubicles (which are in pink, compared to blue) but I stopped them before they get caught.
So I gave up queueing for the showers and I know from outside this shower there's one further down the area (here's the ambiguity), so I packed up my stuff* and went in that direction, assisted by someone in front of me who seemingly is heading in that direction. We entered a mall, turned left and saw toilets. Unfortunately there's a long queue of guys outside the corridor and it looks impossible to queue already.
Then I went back to the original toilet (yes the female toilet) but found people crowding around in front of it. When I walked closer to investigate, I saw some people circling around a middle-aged gentleman with a top hat, in black.
THE INCIDENT
(Did I tell you this was long?)

I asked what happened to the gentleman, and in an array of discrete and small actions (I don't know how to describe them well) - he explained whilst pointing the only objects left on the floor:
"They... (pointed at one object) and... (pointed at another object) and they... (slit throat sound effect)"
(Note: It was much more intense than that. Like a thousand camera angles on like, half a dozen objects. I just don't remember them all...)
As he explains the people around him weep in agony. Also this somehow convinced me that this is most likely a serial murder case. Or not. I wondered if this was just an elaborate trap or some scammer who is out to cheat something from me, probably money. I changed from a face of shock to disbelief.
"Ok I'll take a bet with you that this is not true! If i can find the people back, you're in trouble*. If not (the focus is over here, by the way), I'll apologise to you."

THE LIE
Then I went up the stage nearby to look around the open area. No one I knew from the camp is in sight, even those guys who I know well. I looked back to see the crowd disperse, the gentleman standing up to see around and looked at me with a broad smile. I knew I lost my own bet. (Audience: Haha so quickly?) (Yeah or else it'll take a season of Anime to finish it) I walked past him in disbelief to the toilet I went in in the first place (that female toilet) and noticed a bunch of females and no males, making me notice I entered the wrong toilet. (Audience: It took you this long to notice?) Also, there's a male toilet beside it!
