Itou Miku - Ao 100 Shoku [Single]
Limited Edition Regular Edition
(I mean, clearly the Anime cover is better- If you dare to say that I'll beat you up on behalf of Komi-san)

Origin: You may have heard of voice actress and solo artist Itou Miku from Anime, but this is technically the first time I've ever listened to any of her music apart from her being in an idol group Pyxis with Toyota Moe which I got into only a couple of years ago. Thanks to Anime "Komi-san" though, it's no surprise I may end up with similar artists under the same music label as independent artists from the previous season but despite her being referred to as solo Anime artist, and considering it's her 10th time coming with singles, I would like to mention that this is a good single. And I'll talk about why. Here's a hint, fanservice isn't everything... maybe.

(1) Ao 100 Shoku [100 Colours (of) Blue]
From the first chorus:
"Today I meet you again, 1cm at a time, falling in love
(The) usual times I touch (your) shoulder, 1cm at a time, (the) love wavers
Your gentle heartbeat I have been listening always"
A delightfully good song is probably how I would describe this Anime opening theme song in general, because despite it making a weaker impact in melody compared to its predecessor for Anime opening, its easy listening melody, interesting intro and outro, and specifically I would like to emphasize the Anime-descriptive lyrics are the best points of this song I can think of. I know Miku isn't exactly specialized in hype songs her songs are normally softer, so this is rather adequate for the mood as a potential love song with all the cringes from the Anime, but also, suited to her own solo music style and singing so far.
Rating: Interestingly Above Average

(2) La-Pa-Pa Cream Puff
From the second verse:
"The soft sun spreads, step by step
(It) will show you the way
Shown to (you) is (a) model
Because (we shall) proceed Charming(ly)"
Don't ask me how the title of this song came about, but it won't take you too long to understand how cute this sounds, and especially for a B-side it'll make a better impression than the Anime theme song as A-side. I have to shamelessly say I'm strangely attracted to the lyrics which is just about cream puff with maybe a hint of romance, and how this song is overall presented. It piqued my interest the first time I heard of it, and I don't think this can get any cuter considering the softer vocals we are dealing with too. The only grime I have of this song is the usual bridge part which I must say isn't good, but otherwise this is a catchy melody that you will remember its charm.
Rating: La-Pa-Pa Average

Conclusion: I have to say even in the most unbiassed opinion I can, that this is still a good single. I know I might have been captivated by the softer vocals, the better song compositions considering this is an Anime-related release, but also I would like to be reminded that my tastes in recent years may have geared me to like this release more than I would hope to. If you aren't a fan of Itou Miku before, I have a feeling she might be your new secret admirer from here on. If you are a fan though, not that I have much experience but I did hear some of her earlier songs, and I would say this is definitely up there as one of her top releases of all time. Whether it will stand the test of time though? I wouldn't dare to say, just like how Komi-san wouldn't.


- Aozaki Shouhei, on the Klassic Note
