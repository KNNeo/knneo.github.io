P.S. Because I was lazy to write an actual spec script for an animation movie I intended to do about my own lore for "BLEACH" when I was a student lol, I shall put in point form, where I lie in the history of Soul Society:
  • Soon after the betrayal of Urahara Kisuke, Shihouin Yoruichi and Tsubasaki Tessai and the missing of many captains in Gotei 13 about 100 years ago, Seireitei hastily decided to reform the leaders of Soul Society, namely promoting members of the Squads. For the 12th Squad though, Central 46 was hesitant in allowing the promotion of then Vice Captain Kusotsuchi Mayuri due to his history in prison, thus 3rd Seat Officer Aozaki Shouhei was placed as replacement Captain.
  • Throughout the 5 or so years in reign, Shouhei continued to develop technologies in a very different direction compared to Mayuri, who seeked more occult means of work compared to Shouhei's more practical approaches to improving combat, leading of the widespread use of Kido as one of the four forms of shinigami combat being his vision, and creating training grounds and research facilities powered by Kido so shinigami can train better overall. Most notably, he was able to create a controlled environment to the extent where power-driven 11th Squad members can train their released zanpakuto in.
  • Rumours started to rise within the 12th Squad about Mayuri's doubt on Shouhei's abilities as Captain and Technological Institute Head, not following the vision of technological advancement into souls and moreover prohibiting the experiments Mayuri was doing. In protest, Mayuri discovered that Shouhei has been in close contact with now in Real World Urahara Kisuke, and has been yearning to leave Soul Society to join his master outside of control.
  • The day finally arrived when Shouhei was spotted ready to escape using Mayuri's technologies, though bested by Mayuri's inventions with enough evidence to get him executed, Shouhei fought his way out even revealing his long-trained Bankai on a disadvantage against Mayuri's poison blade. Seireitei was kept mum about the issue especially from Mayuri as this would give him free reign over the rule of the Squad and Institute, in which he eventually obtained.
  • Shouhei in the Real World used a self-made gigai and spent time in hiding before eventually settling down on opening a dojo, where he continued to hone his sword skills since he can't use his spiritual energy. He eventually was able to use all his shinigami power and train a squad of 5 human-shinigami hybrids in the Real World, with Asauchi stolen from Seireitei itself.
