ANIME
Subete ga F ni Naru: MAN! WHY THEY WANT TO MAKE US WATCH THE KILLING!

Anyways, it's another episode. They (sort of, in lack of bikini or some fanservice in the middle of the series whatsoever) took a break from the incident by leaving the lab, and then went back. Well at least now more know about the incident, and that is kind of against the lab's management to not reveal the deaths within seven days,  now that I think about it lol. Also, they found new clues. All the things in Magata-sensei's room is arranged only in stacks of 15 (bookshelf), which explains the "F" in "Fifteen", apparently. Going by that, we get to confirm that in order for Shiki to leave the parents and live her own way she killed her parents along the witness from the uncle and his wife. Man, WHY? But then again, this is as much as we know of for now. Now that we can confirm certain stuff, let's see if any of us (don't include me please) can solve this before the police come, which is going to be like, the 2nd last episode probably.

My hypothesis (not in view of the spoiler I read) still stands that the killer is (1) someone who knows the family and the operation of the lab well, which can explain the "rigging" of the lift, (2) may have been told of the incident 15 years ago (I'll not be surprised if it's one of the alive ones), and (3) is still within the premise because no one has left the island thus far.

MANGA
BLEACH: So... apparently Nanao-chan is going to be a nun? Quite befitting if we have to NOT talk about fighting with a sword. So finally we understand the back story, and I feel very natural due to the fact that Nanao-chan is permitted from using a sword than actually does not possess one. I guess the curse also comes from possessing such a zanpakuto, and thus inhibits her from having her own? Well all that maybe confusing, but next chapter maybe her maiden fight and probably most epic yet. Well if it's a holy sword that cannot kill directly I guess that would be reasonable. I cannot believe I'll say this, but I'll be looking forward to Nanao-chan kick some ass next chapter. HELL YES!
All other manga I actively read: They're not out. And I don't know why. But I'm patient, for I cannot afford to buy the Japanese ones and also not understand even if I bought them.
