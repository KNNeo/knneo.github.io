Hey, it all comes around. So yeah, the ancestor didn't live long, that's for sure, despite Senko-san's care all those years, and Sora is afraid history will just repeat himself with the probably cursed family that Nakano is in. アニメ Shingeki no Kyojin Season 3 Part.2
"Next time, write us an article on how impressive the Scouts are will do" Haha Levi you smart ass on the other hand they'll need it
Man, now I want Erwin to slap you in the f**king face you bloody recruit what the f**k do you know. *laughs* (That idiot knows Erwin rejected the shot?) In this final episode (59) of the season, it brings us one important question: why did Erwin not take the shot FUCK YOU MILITARY PEASANTS haha anyways it's "what should we do now we know of the truth" to be honest. After Eren knew of the deepest of truths, the Scouts at the height of war were honoured with medals, which of course, comes to the displeasement of the rest of the military since... well, they don't know of the truth, for starters. Add in that one noob that survived the war, staying by Erwin the commander till the end, it's no wonder he thinks he's the hero and should be saved. Heroes shouldn't be saved, you idiot. On the other hand, it's not about agreement or anything, but with Eren's whole knowledge of the situation (as we do too, being the audience) he asked himself the ultimate question of this series, on what to do next. Fighting is an option, but if we think about this pragmatically the Eldians here won't stand a chance to the regime that is on the other island, for sure. Then how? Allow themselves to be eradicated even as Zeke goes back to report to the other humans who will then plot their next move to the now shrinking race who knows of the truth? So yeah, it is quite a conundrum already, to say the least. Add in that the people within the Walls got to enjoy a period of peace even after knowing of the truth, and the Scouts being able to explore beyond the walls and not be killed by a bunch of Titans straight on... everything Eren saw in his connecting dreams with the other Titans was revealed in front of their eyes. The Paradise exists to send fellow trapped Eldians who misbehave to the Walls, and the main trio finally got to see the sea. But especially with Eren, he is scared. Scared of the truth. Mostly also because blame was upon them, and they are setting out to...? I mean, if you ask me fighting definitely sounds fun, but they are not going to win against technology and a minigun probably lmao. But considering Titans were the best they can send to the Walls to "fight", I doubt they have progressed much. Many of these would be answered in - I assume, and I hope you have seen it - the final season which will air next year, and I can't wait for an awesome conclusion. I must say, this series has thus far presented itself to be surprising in terms of backstory, and I'm still rather glad it took this long to build it up. All in all, it's filled with action, mostly despair, but as we continue to see hope, we also continue to be aware of the dangers these guys would be in, and I am expecting a sad ending to conclude this series we all enjoyed. Prove me wrong, Anime, and we shall see how this goes on the real war between human races next year. *clenches fist on chest*
