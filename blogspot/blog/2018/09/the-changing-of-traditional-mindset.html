The changing of traditional mindset feels like an event within itself: You change for the better knowing that you'll lose something that holds dear as part of history.

Oh, no one thinks like that anymore? What era am I in? But seriously, guys. Remember the past, stay in the present, anticipate the future. Change, but not forgetting your roots. Radical change isn't part of human adaptability anyways.
