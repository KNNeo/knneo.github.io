Dagashi Kashi
The new episode (2) talks about the very familiar episode which I recall from reading the manga (and I swear I've mentioned it before here last year), so I'll talk about the differences in style of doing this now compared to the Anime. Firstly, the story is about a typhoon day, where it's too dangerous to go back home for the Endou siblings (and Hotaru probably), so they had a sleepover. Simple enough, and this also took only a couple of chapters in the manga, but I'm glad on how far they made it good. Because secondly in animation, they may have cut off quite a few parts to cut short the length of the episode in compensation of completeness but it's done well. For example (spoilers if you didn't read the manga) Tou-kun was seen at home alone, and of course, should be the last person to arrive at the shop. Hotaru was supposed to be seen on the streets and talking about the exhilaration from being outside, but that is not in too. Also, they're most definitely not going to show what happened to Yo-san when he went to his neighbour's place instead of going back home. All in all, I think it's fine, because they did make it consistent enough (Sayashi monologue at the end) to make sure it sounds correct. Adaptation, people. It all makes sense. Still, never get sick of Hotaru fanservice, so despite me covering my eyes when I can see her bra, make sure to keep them coming haha
