"It's as if you are monopolising Eren's power for yourself" Oh so I assume you know what it is? Haha these idiots are so dumb
Wow that's quite a lot of truth to digest after one peaceful episode. The new episode (40) talks about the story behind the government, and how the royalty came to be. According to most accounts from Erwin, Historia's past and the father, we can come up with a pretty good deduction. Allow me to paint the inglorious history for you guys. Story goes - as seen from episode 1 - that a century ago, humans were forced to be behind walls with the emergence of Titans. In there, they hid behind the walls while maintaining prosperity, keeping what's left of the human species alive. Well, at least they's what they tell you. We now know that the government of the Trost district/country/kingdom had to take severe measures to ensure peace within the walls. I mean, why be so extreme with its internal affairs? Why only do the Titans attack at episode 1? Remember there was total peace before that? So yeah, something must have happened with the government itself, and apparently, it was due to the then King who was not meant to be. At this point, the relation between humans in Trost and Titans felt more like a threat, but yeah, the current governing body is balancing between keeping its people alive and quiet while making sure they all don't get killed. Too bad with Eren that might not be possible. Eren's Titan powers might likely happen thanks to the new knowledge of intelligent Titans (read the eyecatch ie. image in the middle of the episode for details) where it is possible for a Titan to get stronger by consuming another. Chances are Eren's dad got hold of such a sample (don't ask me how) and "fed" it to Eren, resulting in his warcry-like Titan powers, which obviously could prove useful in battle. Considering this, the current government would like to of course, take Eren in and you know, NOT make him the superpower. You know, like, let him get eaten by probably someone who is within the walls and knows how to control that power already. Lastly, I'll end this story with supposed plans of Erwin and Levi's squad right now. Erwin aims to seek for the truth to the point of sacrificing for the greater good, and Levi... let's just say his cowboy-looking brethren is getting in the way.
