The power to influence is as important of a state of freedom as the power to be influenced.

I'm not going to elaborate much here, but if you don't believe what society and people around you say, you don't have to take it in. We're not sponges. We can have an opinion, even if that rejecting reality will lead to your change in understanding of the world.
