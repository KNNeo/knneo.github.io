"Why do we work so long and so hard for? To achieve our goals and get all the problems that come with it!"

I mean, that's life, right? The obesity issue is reaching a record high in my country, and I'd rather think that we will never have the time to maintain our body, or even to say, desire to maintain it. And this extends to the many things we do with our money, like spending on luxury and many other things that are in direct consequence of it.

This is going to be life moving forward, people. Live with it. Or take action, if it really matters to you. We can only matter that many things in life.
