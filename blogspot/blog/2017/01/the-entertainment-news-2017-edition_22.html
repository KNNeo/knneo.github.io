Kuroshitsuji: And yes, we're close to yet another conclusion. You know I like a solved mystery.
This latest chapter (125) really talks a lot about the plot, after Sebastian allowed the news stand to just publish the photo out front. Also, if you want me to draw parallel differences to modern medicine today, please read up on blood types and blood transfusion. So, firstly, things we already knew that the Scotland Yard will take years to find out otherwise. There is an obvious lack of supply of a specific type of blood, and if you did learn some blood types you'd know the least amount of blood that exists in the world currently is now the AB blood type, which is likely also the Sirius type as mentioned in Bravat's terms. And if you need more without reading it up yourself, it's because blood type talk about proficiency and deficiency of specific types of chemicals inside one's blood, and AB just happens to be the one that has both missing (A and B), with O being the most common and most usable blood type. This has got to do with blood transfusion as mentioned by Ciel too as blood transfer has a rejection rate, and with a similar type of blood transfused the rejection rate will decrease. This chapter brings us to who finally needs such a blood supply, namely rich guests who are better off dead anyway, and Bravat has yet to reveal a special guest who needs it most, apparently. (Of course, he/she is likely Sirius blood type) Next chapter will likely be the conclusion I guess, and I can't wait to see that bloke fortune teller and his downfall.
