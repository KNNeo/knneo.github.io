Remember: Part of being free is to not know of the truth.

I say this in light of any form of gossip, of course, mostly negative. But hey, that's the problem of being in a community. The truth is, that people can be talking behind your back when moving out as a group about you being a burden, you being too forced on the group etc. but all in all this is inevitable. We just have to be more sensitive towards such action and just... tolerate your own case once in a while. Okay, maybe more than that.
