I want to be able to forget. To forget that I was of importance to someone in their lives.

I know what you're thinking, but it's not with obligation, this one. I'm talking about relationships, from friends to family (well I guess they're exceptions) and everything in between. Maybe it's because I remember too much of me and wasn't socialising enough, I seem to know a lot of myself and not a lot of others, and thus makes me remember everything of others and vice versa, because it was so little compared to me and myself.
