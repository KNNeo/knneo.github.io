ANIME
ACCA 13th District: (I wish it was "Ansatsu-ka" but I've got enough of that octopus already lol. Yeah, got nothing to do with that. FYI it's "Kansatsu-ka", ie. Investigations Lesson, in plain translation. We're going to learn a big lesson here right now)
And indeed we do. (Haha sorry for writing the intro literally starting of watching the episode) The new episode (9) probably is the most groundbreaking of all, of all strategies that is of higher regard, and this one tops it a notch. First half talks about people eyeing for Lotta-chan, knowing that she is of royal blood, and may be of threat to the family. Fortunately this got stopped good by 1x Maggie and his trusted friend, and of course Jean's superior, who immediately reported to ACCA headquarters. Problem here is the second half of this episode. Everything was a plan. A plan above one too, which is just bizarre. Never before have I watched this much layer of shrewdness lay before me (I mean, I probably did, but this one fits perfectly) so quick, and so smoothly. Grossular finally talks about his plans for ACCA to the Chiefs, and Lilium for once agrees with him. Thinking of it as such a good plan, little do we know of Lilium's true identity. I don't think we need further elaboration, but we can see who's on top of who now. Yeah, and among all obstacles they have to clear, Mauve is going to be the next. Will she comply, or will we see a real change in paradigm? Oh man, I can't wait.

Sangatsu no Lion: I think I may have teared up too much for this episode. But seriously, is this what happiness feels like?
This new episode (21) finally takes the break off that tournament, and brings us a lot of... happiness, it seems. We start with Shimada and his hometown Yamagata, and hell yeah the elderly welcome him as usual, despite him being a rain-bringer haha. Nonetheless, the events held on his hometown are meaningful, including Human Shougi (didn't even know this was a thing) and 100-Move Matches (I guess it's just pros playing with the people) which are interesting in its own way. Hearing Shimada's story about contributions he did to his hometown also felt great. No wonder he was so concerned about answering to his club. Second half talks about the Kawamoto family coming up with a new item, and hell yeah Hinata got the idea. Despite them having to spend a bomb on a sweet shop she still managed to think of something new. Such cohesiveness, such team-building... I wished I had a family like that. And of course not forgetting Kiriyama, he's there just on time too.
Too much of a masterpiece I can't help but put aside all the jokes for this haha

Demi-chan wa Kataritai: This new episode (10) we talk about Dullahans. Like, a lot more about it. Like, we have to go to a university and talk to a professor type of level. What's more, a physics pro. What's more, talk about wormholes! Hey, I know this is not a scientific experiment of whatever sort, but I swear if the author has a hard time writing this or everything else in this story (it may not be an either-or though) I won't blame him. Otherwise, you'd be pleased to hear Machi-san may just be an epitome of time-space research! Well, maybe not now, but it sounds like it from an expressionless person lol. Anyways, it's interesting and all, and hey we shouldn't forget Takahashi-sensei's kindness and Machi-san's desperation for a kiss of some sort. Haha, you'd wish the succubus could do something about that.
No, it's not audio sync problems. He was genuinely excited.
(Wow I have a feeling this makes the team a lot happier animating this guy haha)
P.S. Machi-san MVP of this episode? Well, Hikari-chan may be best girl, but still.
