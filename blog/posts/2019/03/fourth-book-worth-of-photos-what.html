THE INTERVIEW
For the interview, since this is the one - of those which I intentionally avoided for the other three (well, by chance) - that has such a thing, I'll try my best to note down in point form some of the highlights about her life till now with some photobook scoops:
  • She was born 22nd February as 2nd child of the family, and it was a smooth childbirth, as mentioned by the mother. (lol TMI)
  • Marinka as a kid is troublesome (lol). She gets lost easy so "Marika-chan is missing" is a common phrase running in the family (lol why)
  • Both her parents work and she plays hide and seek with them .(haha what a kid)
  • She transferred schools before when young, so her in elementary school is basically lone wolf. As soon as she makes friends she gotta wave them goodbye already. (I wouldn't haha but then again probably no phones yet?)
  • It continued until middle school so the library was her friend. (No wonder the librarian fantasizing haha) But even then she had friends, many didn't stay with her until she announced this photobook to them and they be like "oh" (well so much for even just classmates)
  • She got interested in the seiyuu line of work since elementary school (no wonder, from above) and Sundays watching Anime with family became a thing. (I want to watch Anime with my parents- well forget it haha) She noticed the similar voices who acted back then in those animations. (I'm not going to list the name here haha)
  • Even then she didn't think of being one of them. She still liked books so Anime wasn't really a thing in her life yet. She slowly tried to voice dialogues or just read off from the books, and even did it in front of her mother. (Kawaii...)
  • As she got interested in such a line of work and didn't know what it meant, at the end of middle school her mother told her to "fill in your future for beyond high school" and since she didn't really know she chose what she thought she did best, and that is voice acting since her mother liked it. (Aw...) Since reading of books was a thing, seeing the link towards seiyuu as a career became the natural thing.
  • In reading of books, her image of it was of a minstrel, and thought seiyuu might be a similar line of work. However as she continued on her journey she noticed that it wasn't the case, and as much invested as she is at her job now, she still liked the idea of "conveying stories to people" and took that as her impression today.
  • With that in mind, she entered voice acting academy to start her career as a seiyuu, (thanks to her mother's questioning of her future) and soon found herself voice acting the second day she entered a talent company after graduation. And it also featured recording for an Anime theme song? Wow.
  • Uma Musume was her most deep-rooted series she starred in, as it expressed a lot of her acting in many ways than other and the reputations and titles she got from within the community in Japan.
  • She did think about the future, like getting a bigger house for the family (#GoodKidGoals) as she continues to think for the welfare for people around her.
  • She doesn't have desire for marriage (why) mostly because she can't imagine spending millions of dollars on kids and raising them until they are 20 (direct quote btw) and worrying them here and there... she thinks it's rather impossible for her to perceive at the moment. (Hey, when you're rich... haha)
  • [On photobook comments] That white one-piece was her image of pureness in this photobook. And then she just wants to play around in that outfit (lol). She quickly noticed that she has to preserve that ladylike image as of the outfit (haha #ProblemKid)
  • The scene with her on the tatami was reminiscent of her thinking about a grandmotherly image (as in being at your elderly's place) as she plays games and relaxed near the porch.
  • The cat was called "Kuu-chan" and part of the ryokan (traditional hotel), it got in the way often so it became part of the photobook literally became MVP (lol)
  • She thought she would go with an image she saw from a model when she had to do the bath scene (lol) by donning on some sort of clothing and entering in but then it just became her getting into the bath and nothing else. Of course she had a bath towel wrapped on her (what did I tell you) (DAMMIT haha) but the feeling of water coming out from her sides and between gaps (hell yeah) really made her feel embarrassed. (it was a photo shoot so she wasn't alone btw) But then the scenery from the bath outside (I'll kill all the snoopers for you Marinka lol) and her drinking the champagne in the bath made her feel like "oh I'm living the rich man's life now" and it felt good (lol)
  • [On the interviewer asking if she is embarrassed with just a bath towel] (man, this guy haha) With the bath towel that has plenty of "protection" (ie. it's thick haha) so it felt like wearing a swimsuit in a hot pool? It felt more like roomwear but it also felt more like her true self (you wear bath towels in your room??) which in turn made her embarrassed.
  • She also tried to show some mature side of her in the photobooks in the streets to make it feel like an "adult-like summer" (yeah, that bath scene is good enough haha) with the shooting. The cat was male, to her shock, and it ended up as a "mixed bath" (wait, what?)
  • When she was playing around with the beach ball, it got washed away by the waves and back so it was between "we can't save it" and "over here over here", waving her hands and eventually she (likely it's a "they" there's staff around) saved it and she happily hugged it in fear of it being washed away again. (I want to be the ball haha)
  • When she hit the bamboo stick during the watermelon splitting, it broke the stick instead of opening the fruit (lol) so it became a "takewari" instead of a "suikawari"
  • She freaked out seeing a crab (not shown in photobook) and the wind was great at the rocks. It just had a lot of bugs (haha) so it felt close to the concept for summer, shooing away the bugs (haha #RealityCheck)
  • [On her monthly serialisation] She thought of being baseball club manager (yay Anime scenarios), has never touched a surfboard before during that shoot, and she liked the nobility shots.
  • It then went a little off into impersonations in fictional stories, talking about classics from Momotaro to western tales, letting her try out a variety of cosplay.
