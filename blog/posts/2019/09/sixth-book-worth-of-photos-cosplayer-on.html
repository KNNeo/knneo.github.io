THE ORIGIN
I'll keep it short because I swear I've mentioned her in this place a couple of times. I'll refer you to her Twitter account for all the other information, but to summarise Tachibana Haru is a regular cosplayer, sometimes MC, sometimes casual model and everything in between. Quite a beauty talent herself despite the height (haha sorry), she started to accumulate her popularity with a goal of becoming a Race Queen(RQ) in Japan and playing games and appearing as guests as seen in other recent games too if you ever took notice of anything else surrounding it besides playing the damn game. I knew her from mobile game "Alternative Girls" back in 2016 (still playing it) and the official livestream featured her and another guy (some ripped dude who left) ("Muscle Miyazaki", they call him) playing the games and talking strategies and so on. As the game gained attention, I also took myself some time to watch the sub-4 hour livestream on average only to be amazed by her and her rapport with the others, displaying her hearty character and sometimes cheeky personality. It eventually died down with the closure of the series 2 years in the run, and was only later in the year revived with another regular livestream series featuring popular hosts which of course, did not include her.

Along the way, I thought she was lovely so I followed in her career into the (doujin) entertainment industry, I voted her online two years straight which in the end gave her the prestigious RQ title, and... I went to insult her avatar when she hosted her first solo livestream playing Mario Kart. *laughs* (I seriously didn't expect her to see my comment in the 100 people who were in it and again, I'm sorry) All in all, it was quite a fun 3 years and beyond, as she continues to grow, find a talent company to stay in which coincidentally stemmed from the producers of that gaming livestream, and it came to this. When she announced she was going to make her last Comiket photobook, I knew my chance was coming. (#YearOfThePhotoBook lol) Since I can't be there to shake her hand or hug her (she probably will be okay with that maybe) I had to patiently see Comiket end with her booth (only available on last day) and wait for her to announce she will be selling them at an online store.
