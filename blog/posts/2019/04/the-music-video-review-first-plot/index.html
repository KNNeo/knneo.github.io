I said I'll never do another one of these again. I guess I broke my own promise haha

So yeah, I may have ditched Attack on Titan (you can see if the Entertainment News post is updated after this lol) just to do this, but I watched the one music video last night and I want to hug Nan-chan so bad but I can't so here we are.

Yes, it's a Natsukawa Shiina music video review!! Don't even stop me from commenting on this hehe


THE PLOT
No pun intended because the title of the song says so, but it's plenty of things fused into one. For one, special effects. If you noticed and if you're like me who saw the making video too, you'll be surprised to see many scenes of her actually running in front of a green screen, which also brings us to the next point. Then, it's a journey back in time. To those music videos she has been in, more like a nostalgia ride, but also a recollection, which is also what the lyrics try to iterate. (Lyrics written by hers truly, by the way) (Apparently, it's a trend somehow) Lastly, it's plenty of close-ups. It's her in glasses, in like a jumper suit, and her being emotional. More than enough to make me love her once more. Okay let's get to the images hehe
