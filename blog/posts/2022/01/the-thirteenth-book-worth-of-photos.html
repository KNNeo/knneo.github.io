THE 2022 CALENDAR
As usual around November of last year, I was searching and also waiting around for some surprises of someone releasing a photobook, and am excited to hear from Kotobuki Minako after her overseas stay in England aka United Kingdom since April 2020 and don't ask how she can even go there lol, two announcements that made my day. She is coming back to Japan after more than a year and a half for her further education in case you don't know of it, but also, she is releasing a calendar book for her stay in foreign land. So yeah, that's how this happened. How they decided that this is a good idea to make it into a calendar with the mask on some photos to remind me where we are in 2022, but hey at least Mina-chan be making content other than YouTube after her long stay overseas. So yeah, I didn't hesitate to give anything else, it was on limited edition pre-order too which happened soon after the previous package got sent here, so I know this will only arrive by the new year which is fine by me.
