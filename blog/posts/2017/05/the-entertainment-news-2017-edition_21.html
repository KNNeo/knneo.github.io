アニメ Alice to Zouroku
THIS IS ALL I HAVE WANTED. Thanks, Anime. Can we just have this for the rest of the season? No?
This new episode (#AliceToZouroku06) after an arc review (see above, and last week) is just... a slice-of-life Anime. In case you're here for just the action and think this is the next magical girl show or the next major arc of fighting and magic or whatever, it's not. At least, not for this episode. And maybe next week too, seeing the title. So yeah, this might just be it. Slice-of-life, the show that I've always wanted it to be. I mean, you don't know how it feels to watch Episode 1 and hope for the day it'll be like Usagi Drop where we talk about family and life, but hey, we apparently are. The episode lightly plays two days worth of life in the storyline, where Sana-chan joins Zouroku in walking the streets, helping out in his florist shop where possible, and getting accepted into the family. Hey, not very surprising, but he did it. Good on him, old man. But hey, as I mentioned, I like myself some peace and quiet. The problem here is, will it stay on like that? Will a bigger problem emerge considering it took 5 episodes to get Sana-chan to settle down? How long will this peace last? We won't find out next episode though it seems, but maybe soon. I don't know when, but soon.

アニメ Eromanga-sensei
"Sorry, but I have siscon!" -Izumi Masamune, 2017 (Yeah, you wish he'd said that to Muramasa haha)
Hey, this new episode (#EromangaSensei07) continues on the potential competition between Masamune and the most popular light novel author above him, Muramasa Senjuu. Problem is, because this is a harem show (if it isn't obvious to you yet), we got another person who is in love with him. Not only for his works (because she probably didn't read enough books lol), but the person himself. (I hope she has seen enough guys to know Masamune isn't the best looking guy around lol) Although I have a feeling this is just a one-hit wonder episode where it's focused on Muramasa herself, but in this case there is also a lot on the flashbacks, and a lot on the life stories. The competition is still up, and she thought she could stop the sibling love that is between Masamune and Sagiri. She was wrong. Sagiri stepped up to the occasion (like, literally) to show how much that story matters to the couple to say the least, and it kind of makes of a slightly expected cliffhanger, because someone couldn't stop writing that love letter haha. On the other hand, in view of this going full on harem in the next few episodes, I would say that even so Muramasa (she) will no doubt play a good role in Masamune's (he) life. And as mentioned by the sister who should probably get out of the house soon, she might just go on dates with the most popular guy in town haha. No seriously, this is LITERALLY the definition of a harem Anime, for those who don't know of this term.

アニメ Shingeki no Kyojin Season 2
I can see how this is going to get exciting after this part now that we have the main party joining in (okay, no Levi, but still), so this season is going to end strong, I can feel it.
This new episode (#AttackOnTitan33) talks about the conclusion, which was whatever happened when the Colossal Titan dropped right on struggling Reiner and Eren in titan form. Turns out Bertolt (I researched on the name haha) was just throwing down his titan form, ready to distract Eren as he gets his limbs bit off by Reiner, before they decide to leave with Eren and Ymir. I won't bore you with the flashback and story afterwards, but suffice it to say many were injured, and waiting for the main party might just be their best option (as I said, no Levi haha). Good thing is, Hannes, who is smarter than those two traitors, knows where they would be headed. As time is of essence, this might just be it. I mean, for this season, to settle things straight.
