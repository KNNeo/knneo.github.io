You cannot rank people you know by anything, but you can label them.

This means a lot when I thought about it because you can label someone your partner but never always (I say with extreme prejudice) say that you love her more than everyone else. No ranking, yes? Giving everyone identities is less biased and more focused, which feels healthier in my opinion.
