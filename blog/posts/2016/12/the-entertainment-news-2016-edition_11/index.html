MOVIE
Lazer Team: Finally it took some time, but I got a chance to sit down and watch this show. If you need a quick opinion, this is nothing short of a AAA title. In detail? You'll have to sit in.

If you need to know I've been watching YouTube videos for a long time, and exactly four years back, I sat on the ship that is Rooster Teeth, that, and their two gaming channels under its name, Achievement Hunter and Funhaus. I know watching people playing games for a living isn't related to this, but this is just the roots. I didn't fund for this show, nor did I watch it live from last year. Besides, this was only a YouTube Red original and only aira on selected areas. As I said, not your usual AAA title that is big enough to make it on every screen. Nonetheless, I'm impressed. Special effects are done right, stunts are executed flawlessly, music and sound effects are totally on spot, camera work is nothing short of the obvious and doesn't make you feel like you're watching - as the Americans say - a college project (ie. professional), the overall plot is well thought out like how an action movie should have, from an unlikely hero to a likely vicitm etc, but hey all's well kind of ends well. And a sequel is still in production as we speak. I'll say it again, I'm impressed. For them, a small company who does YouTube for a living, they have came a long way. And as the "founding fathers" would have envisioned, this one is a good start to what they wanted.

I foresee the sequel to be full-fledged. Then maybe I'll GET MY ASS over to a movie theater to actually watch it. *laughs*
A very appropriate summary of what this movie has to offer haha
P.S. I like how they picture the universe with Earth and aliens. Putting that into my "inspiration for worldview" list
