It was staying at home for me at the end of the year ever since I moved into Tokyo, but not this year it won't be. Takahashi Rie, who I met at my new job? We have been hanging out with each other for the past few months. But it was always because we wanted to avoid the rest of our department - the older co-worker crowd who are still living in the Showa Era - that we chose to hang out together.

This time around, Takahashi-san invited me over to her place for New Year's to hang out with her friends, but as I entered her apartment and put down my stuff, I realized there was no one else. I dared not take a step further into her living room which smelled better than a flowerbed, so I took a seat at a stool near the kitchen. I asked if they has anything planned for this gathering, maybe play some games. She laid down onto a pillow on the cyan-coloured sofa beside me, and looked back at me with a smile and a glint in her eyes.

"What do you want the two of us to do together?"
