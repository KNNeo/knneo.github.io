You think anyone else in this planet viewed her as a younger sister like I would? I hope not haha




A year after my parents divorced, I got a phone call from one of them to meet their new family, specifically my three new younger stepsisters. We met up in a park near the city area. As I arrived, I greeted the new couple and formally introduced myself. I looked around and did not see anyone else. They told me their other daughters were arriving and eldest sister Shiina walked away, pointing me to the wooden bridge in the distance. She was looking away from us, leaning onto the ledge in the middle of the bridge.

I approached her as the sunlight shone towards me, creating a silhouette around her. She was dressed in a set of brown overalls with a white blouse, blending into the scenery. Hearing footsteps on the wooden bridge, she slowly turned her head around to look at me. As I closed my distance and stopped in front of her, I noticed she was shorter and a lot cuter than I expected. That was when I decided to love and protect this new younger sister of mine for the rest of my life.
