We did postpone it to the final day in hopes of the sky clearing up, and we managed to do everything else on our list. As we sat in our hotel on the final day staring out into the dull morning sky, Aoi stood up and decided she wanted to go to the beach! I explained how underwhelming it might be and the possibility of rain again, and she beat my argument with a squeal.

"But I want to go to the beach!"

And so, we took off to one a bus trip away.

As we landed, Aoi rushed out into the beach with her sunhat. The weather did clear up a bit, and the beach was rather empty. The sky brightened our moods, and the sea breeze cheered us up in no time. She looked back at me with a satisfied smile. I was so glad I came out with her to her hometown.
