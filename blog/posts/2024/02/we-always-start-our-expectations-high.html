 Sunday, 04 February 2024
We always start our expectations high, but forget that our talent and skills hardly ever start as high as our expectations.

I always like to talk about adequacy, but instead I will talk about learning to know how far one will fall in any event. Because many people don't, and they fall into an abyss that is hard to come out of. Sure, have enough willpower and any one can overcome it, but are we that strong that we think we are? If not, then maybe it's time to normalised everything you expect and desire to what you know how to control. And I don't mean spending money, you can spend money however you want as long as you have enough.

Coming back though, which is why you start high, because that's when your know where you are in the first place. You did that for your expectations, why not extend that to your talent and skills?
