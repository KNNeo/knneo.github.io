I recalled the first time I had a trip with my cousins years ago, to a summer beach house in Okinawa. Everyone had a great time outdoors, I came back to the abode early to chill. I sat legs crossed on a table with a bowl of oranges, and was reminded by my aunt we were free to eat them. I peeled a couple and ate them slowly. As the sunlight softened and the fan blew a steady breeze on me, I lied down on the tatami floor, closed my eyes while I listen to the scenery of nature, and dozed off.

When I woke up, I felt a piece of blue cloth covering my view and heard someone giggling to my right. I turned my head to see Kouno Marika beside me, with her hands almost touching my face. She was also under the cloth in her skimpy summer wear, looking at me with a smile and squinting eyes.

"(Your) sleeping face is also adorable~!"

We stared at each other for a couple of seconds before I realized what was going on and sat up, lifting the cloth behind me. Kouno-san then laughed and ran away.
