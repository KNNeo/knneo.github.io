THE ORIGIN
Shout out to when I took a dump on Ogura Yui about 2 years ago while explaining who she is to my friend and choosing between her and Kitou Akari:
I know right, this is how far I have come. *giggles*

But yes, my decision to talk about this photobook is not just about where this book is based on, but more of giving chance to Yui-chan here as I seem fit. With retrospect, I have never bought a Toyota Moe photobook (yet) it's as if they stopped releasing photobooks and I followed her longer. Okay coming back, I have known of her in Anime and her solo artist career for years even going as far back as 2017, she sings great songs not going to lie it's always dependent on the song for me. I did take a fancy to her even all those years ago, but seeing a new generation of female voice actresses appearing in Japan and not exactly being enticed by them (mostly due to lack of Anime credit, nowadays it's more of idolism lifting their mobile game-oriented voice acting careers now), somehow it made me take a step back and care about people I knew from Anime, from Sakura Ayane to Takao Kanon, people who have actually been in Anime more often than the majority. At this point, even reaching towards Ishihara Kaori in recent years with me missing her roles in Anime and her songs, I might as well give all the members of StylipS at this point, signalling more of a renaissance of liking them more as they grow older. And this is a sign I've seen in my own tastes so far as I get older too.

As to how all this started though, I was definitely not impressed when she did her new single last November even with a livestream I did watch because of YouTube algorithms, but shortly after that, she announced a new photobook which I wasn't too keen on it (since she did release a bunch of them when she... was... younger lol), until I saw that this time almost 3 years since her previous photobook, she is taking a trip to do this in Singapore!

We should be familiar of the city state in the Southeast Asia region and if you don't I might as well give a summary as a loyal citizen I am *dabs*, because *reads propaganda* our strategic location as a trade hub, global financial district and in recent years a tourist destination, definitely helped shape how many people see us on the world stage, even attracting Anime events to be hosted in this very place, with most infamous Marina Bay Sands located in the newly developed city area where it's bringing in ALL the idols. From Uesaka Sumire last year for an Anime festival and eating laksa in the high-end district which probably costed more than my lunch and dinner (OOF), to Kitou Akari's recent tour for Anime "Kimetsu no Yaiba" walking around the largest indoor garden with maintenance so high you need to pay to go in? Knowing that Yui-chan is up and around months ago is not likely going to get me to hunt her down (lol), but it's at least comforting to know that they have been closer to me than ever before. Distance, I meant physical distance, I don't get chicks and we all know that. *silence*

I did go through quite a process and hesitated quite a lot in order to make this purchase, seeing all that I have going for likely is just talking about my home country, but also on all the store-specific free bromide photos that I've always chased even after all these years, this was no exception. I initially wanted to bundle this photobook with the new voice actress magazine which featured Takahashi Rie and Yomiya Hina in which the latter can reinforce my talk about walking out of my usual list of idols too, but as time went by and stock went out, it was harder to make such a decision considering that the latter has been leaked online already showing rather lack of content (I will still buy on digital maybe?), so I knew to change my plans. I also ended up not buying from TSUTAYA ONLINE or Rakuten Books due to how hard it is to sign up to buy from them (ie. you need a Japanese phone number verification), and ended up choosing Gamers as my accomplice (lol) because they are still running a free shipping campaign with minimum purchase... which will be hit if I buy something else with it so be sure to check out last Friday's post on what you have missed. *giggles*

At this point, I honestly don't know what to expect of this book or how far Yui-chan here will go to show her slender body (hehe how do I even know), so we shall see what's up! I mean, it's going to be Singapore talk, amirite fellas?? She's just secondary. *laughs* Ok ok let's open her up. *dabs*
