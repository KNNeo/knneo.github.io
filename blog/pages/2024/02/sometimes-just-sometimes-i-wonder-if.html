Sometimes, just sometimes, I wonder if I've robbed someone of their dream for just being alive.

Watching back on Anime movie "JoseeTora" with Kumiko the main girl strapped onto a wheelchair, she has big dreams even though she's stuck at home most of the time. I wonder if for me being physically fit(?), among other things, that I've not done much compared to someone who isn't who will have bigger goals and dreams than me.

Maybe, just maybe that's why, I should be involved in charity more.
