HOW THIS CAME TO BE
Wow where should I even start... I guess as with all introductions, I think I liked her the moment I saw her from the introductory livestream from "IDOLY PRIDE" back in 2019 *oof* almost 5 years ago this love, and if you would want to argue with me I'll take you on haha, among the 10 new...ish voice actresses announced to be in the series split into two idol groups, she is definitely the cutest of them all. *wink* We will not talk about everyone else in that livestream since I did end up liking the bunch of them eventually *ahem* MusicRay'n 3rd gen *ahem* but this isn't about them, it's about Kanno Mai right here.

Initially I did not gain any interest in the Anime series which aired in 2020 or the mobile game that came out half a year later especially when the developers behind those ruined their previous game which I liked for this (and we shall not talk any more on that lol *sob*), but suffice it to say she did catch my attention some time later if I'm not wrong around 2021 from an online livestream series "Seiyuu to Yoasobi" (as a guest, for her mail role in "IDOLY PRIDE", no surprises) which is also when I saw she is rather active on Instagram, specifically doing acapella livestreams (not archived btw), where she sings her favourite songs to even taking in fan requests. I ended up following her for about 3 years now, getting into the flow of watching her social updates over time, getting into her singing even if it doesn't ring any bells, because damn, her appeal really set onto me if it hasn't onto you. One would even argue that "cute" is the ONLY thing I can describe her during this period.

Her next break came in not because of Anime because why would they these days girls just be cute and be popular lol she is one of those by the way, is with the introduction of her niconico channel (nicknamed "MaiMild", for full name see photobook title), where she hosts a weekly midnight-airing radio show which I listen every Monday making sure I get tired more than once a day for the work week *giggles*, and a monthly subscription which includes access to her once in a month video livestream where she eats stuff and tease us both (lol) and do cute stuff hehe... it was an offer hard to resist. Till then, she has been my first and only reason to (1) sign up for a niconico channel subscription of which its series is responsible for this photobook itself, (2) paid for her birthday livestream where she sings cover songs in a frilly pink outfit of which I watched live AND watched its archive every day until its removal, and (3) at this point so far paid the most amount of money into a photobook she announced a couple of months ago after teasing it during that very birthday live.

Man, I hope this is a good enough summary I assure you there will be many revisions of this *giggles*, but hey, I think this is adequate to describe the wonderful-genius-adorable perfection that is Kanno Mai for now. *dabs* (Yes that phrase has a meaning too lol)

THE INTERVIEW
Since this one had one and as with all of them (exceptions included lol), and this one only spanned two pages so I guess it's no harm for her to talk about definitely not her childhood and voice acting career LMAO, because this interview is mostly about how all this came to be, from her perspective.

Here is a brief summary of what she had to say, with my comments in brackets:
  • Titled "Getting charged in Okinawa! Working hard from here on at my pace!!", by the way (I CAN HEAR HER SAY THAT ALREADY lol)
  • She overall enjoyed the photo-taking trip to Okinawa. She got real surprised to visit the cactus farm for herself (haha ah hey), and was taught that these also grow in winter season. She was a 30-time victim to mosquitoes there though. (OOF)
  • She said the denim outfit did give out a K-pop (fashion) feel with the white boots and with the help of genius makeup artist on the stylistic eyeliners, which made it even more impressionable.
  • This visit of a cactus farm was inspired by fans loving the "cactus-like" poses of her (so far only) main role character in "IDOLY PRIDE" as Kawasaki Sakura, which gave her a reason to visit.
  • With her China outfit greatly inspired by her favourite character in Anime "Pretty Cure" series "Cure Black" (yes, the initial series, I actually watched that a bit as a kid) with the black dress and cute frills, along with the cute bun hairstyle made her trip to Chinese-inspired "Fukushuuen Garden" with the patios and rivers, all the more perfect.
  • On her trip to the "American Village" (this is a thing btw), since the shooting was close to Christmas season (we were hinted that she did the shooting end of the year so no wonder), all their photo shots were to showcase the lightings, she even learnt a lot of shooting techniques from the cameraman themselves, knowing which angle to take, where to take them, it even impressed the staff.
  • She also went for a very casual feel for this part of the shoot, and a rather boyish outfit with short pants and sneakers and not what everyday her would be wearing, especially with the yellow colours kicking in a fresh feel with the lazy-like hairstyle. She ended up buying the white cap for herself.
  • "Kanno Mai proven to be a woman who brings in good weather during the shooting!?" (lol)
  • The bed was so comfortable, the sunlight was so nice and warm, she admitted she did almost fall asleep during the shooting *laughs*. For this part of the shoot though she relied a lot on the staff, which gave her many firsts including wearing pants cut off to show knees and not wearing makeup for the first time on camera! (I assume this is much shame for her since she only appears with makeup sometimes even too much) Paired with the pants, it was a style never seen before, and she felt it to be refreshing. And she wasn't lying when she said it's without makeup: She asked the staff if it was okay to just style her hair a bit and that's all. (Yes HAHA) (Well, there you go, she said it herself)
  • On her trip to "Rukyu Village" specifically on the soba noodles house there, she wore a popular trendy "Big Silhouette" T-shirt (looks like a ZOZOTOWN sub-brand from a quick search online) with a different pleats hairstyle! The soba was delicious and it warmed her up. (I assume the weather at this time of shooting was cold despite the closer-to-Equator location)
  • When inside the village, the theme was to treat the shooting as if it was "the granddaughter from Tokyo came to visit" type of feel, she just called the cameraman "obaa-san" (lmao) all the time. She did a lot of active stuff running and jumping, and they took photos of that too. She crafted some sheet magnets of the Okinawan dog, and since she chose a pink one and painted it with girlish green eyebrows, she named it "GalSa" (lol the animal was called "shisa").
  • She also went to (aptly named) "Southeast Botanical Gardens" for the illumination and lighting over there, had totally different feels seeing a garden light up at night, and took photos of the Christmas tree and the Okinawan dog (statues) there. Thanks to the makeup artist's genius work, despite the same face (lol yeah no shit) we got to see a different feel of her in the makeup and the (matured) outfit.
  • The second outfit where she revisited the "American Village" had cat ears for her winter cap and arm warmers which gave out such a cute feel, she ended up buying the cap as well.
  • She enjoyed the fried chicken she had over there (in the village) especially eating it with onions, it was so delicious she bought a fried chicken keychain before she left.
  • Shout out to the interviewer asking about the feeding pose because she felt ashamed about doing it, but felt like it was to portray the scene at the time, to show how well it tasted. (RARE EXHIBITION OF SHAME!!! Haha)
  • The photo shoot on the beaches of "Senaga Island", along a nearby visit to "Toyosaki Seaside Park", was just perfect for the white dress and ponytail as they paint perfectly into the scenery, she felt like it was a dream come true. There was small rain when they got there but it was gone by the time she stepped into the sea. People kept disbelieving when she said she is a woman who brings in good weather, but this is something she thinks she can convince us of now. Thanks to that, we got beautiful shots of soft winds blowing her hair below the beautiful clouds.
  • (Not going to make fun of her calling her "Moses" for controlling the weather since the interviewer did it lol kawaii)
  • Overall, she enjoyed her time for the photo shoot. The food was great, and she had blue shaved ice a lot of times, even in the cold *laughs*. Also the chinsuko and baked purple potato... (not going to go through all)
  • In a sentence, she thought the food was good and the scenery was beautiful! She thought Okinawa will be a place with varied seasons, but still enjoyed the winter season when she was there. Everyone was happy, time flows slowly, it felt like a happy place to be.
  • She actually wanted to go to Okinawa and Hokkaido for work reasons (if possible), and even travel overseas as she did not have experience in the latter. She wants to see Aurora (lights) in the flesh, even going to see the Aurora belts will also be a nice thing to do when she retires.
  • On to talking about the series "MaiMild", she said it has been about a year (as of photo shoot), and she gained a lot of experience from it. Things that she wanted to do eventually, it all got fulfilled. (I hope it's not all the shameful lines she had to say lmao) Doing a birthday event, singing in it too, wearing a princess outfit, an all pink outfit... because of the support from the staff, she felt more grateful than ever.
  • When asked what she want to eat from here on considering she does that on every (video) livestream every month, she said instead of eating she would like to cook for once, and wondered if that is fine. (Yes but...) Maybe making dishes with coriander, or with fan votes on what to make (alright the former one is NOT one of them lol her obsession of that vegetable is absurd FYI). Or just making them without coriander would be great too.       *laughs*
  • When asked about how the image (from the staff) of KBS symbolizing dropping of stuff, she... uh, thought the staff was saying bad things about someone else? (lol why) But later mentioned that she didn't spill anything during her time at Okinawa. She did screw up something that she bragged about in the radio show though, mentioning that she built a fence as tall as her by herself but she ended up taking 5 hours assembling it (lmao), did not realize the glue she used with the screws(?) was on her bed, still determined to finish it only to notice it was still in the bed when she slept that night!
  • When asked what happened to the bag of butamen that was given to her (as a hoax from where she spilled one during the first livestream), she mentioned that she didn't touch on it much, and only went as far as buying a butamen keychain. She did get hungry right afterwards though so she had it with coriander, and it was alright. (Haha why) She preferred if there was seafood on it or curry (it was base flavoured too which I assure you is not the most popular one even to me), had 6 of them left over (as of the interview), and was welcome for anyone who wants it. (Hey, if you give this to a fan during an event? They will *scream* haha just a suggestion)
  • When asked about the series' most popular segment "YES or NO" (basically a commited Q&A speedrun where she can't lie is the problem), she denied its popularity entirely indicating it as a "NO". (*laughs*) She does try her best in these segments because fans like it... BUT!! She didn't feel that everyone is enjoying it, and she felt that she was suffering, so it's a complicated feeling, but she did enjoy doing it so *fake laughs*
  • She hesitated when told if she wants to measure her height once again this year since the time she did it in a livestream measuring at 144.5cm (shorter than her profile reported). She said that she will eat more and grow taller to prepare for this. (Sure dude, if that's how you think that works lol)
  • She knows it takes a lot of effort to write handwritten mails to her, and she felt happy when she gets to read and talk about these stuff on the radio show. Also all the mail she got for her birthday event she brought all of them back home, and shed tears of happiness as she read through all of them. 
  • She also did "MaiCatch" eyecatch segments during the livestream (for the livestream, they will play some of them back on stream once in a while) and she recalled especially during the live recording event back in June (2023) she understood the prompt wrongly which ended up being edited with the logo flying around to cover up for her staring up in the wrong direction while doing a handshake pose. (Wow I wasn't there did that really happen? lol)
  • The new stuff she intends to try out in the series (I assume on the video livestream) are communicating with fans more (in different ways), and since fans wanted more singing events she wants to give them more opportunities to hear them, and maybe someday make an original song for the series, which will be a dream fulfilled to her.
  • When asked about her ambitions of being a voice actress from here on, she admitted she has a lot she wants to do, it has been such a childhood goal for her, she wants to continue doing it still. She said though she is (indeed) more involved in musicals and recitals, she does not mind expressing herself wholly (I assume, as in full on acting) as that would be great. She liked overseas dramas and movies, and would like to continue voicing for them. (Did she even?) It has also been a big dream of being able to voice act a main role in an Anime she also sings the theme song for. (*looks at Kana-chan*) (again) (haha ikr)
  • In her final message (in this book) to fans, she thanked them for the little things they do to support her, she felt her presence was heard. Even meeting them in person has been a miracle, she has only been blessed for the support so far. She did in times, for example in online talk events, get noticed by staff that she wasn't as energetic as she (her image) would've been as fans were worried from just feeling her (vibes), she appreciated such love that this (feeling of) "wonderful-genius-cuteness" has been conveyed to everyone. With such support, she felt closer than ever to her fans.
  • She also had a message for her buddy Nyanko-chan, but worried that the alligator toy (which quickly became her toy for that one livestream lol) might take over her position as supportive/supported character for the series, and thanked them for (always) being there. Touching it and the face especially felt great (lmao TMI), and she was glad it had a space in the photobook.
