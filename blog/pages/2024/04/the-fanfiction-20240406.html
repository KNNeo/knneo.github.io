We took a stroll along the path, taking in the light pink hue blending into the pale blue sky. Sora then walked near a cherry blossom tree branch and put one of the flowers close to her face.

"The sakura are beautiful, isn't it?"

"The sky is beautiful, isn't it?"

Her smiling mouth opened up into a surprise after hearing my reply, with a blush deeper than the cherry blossom petals around her.
