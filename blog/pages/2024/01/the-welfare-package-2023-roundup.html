P.S. I did some research ie. I just searched in Japanese lol, the difference between the two terms of photobooks used in Japan which are in Kanji "shashinshuu" and in Katakana "fotobukku". The gist is that the latter is for expressing something so it typically comes with comments and more focused on its art, and the former is more of just purely showing off photos. Which will explain why Koko-chan's books are so far called the latter term just because- You saw that cover on the 2nd photobook though? That's expression, I'll give you that.
