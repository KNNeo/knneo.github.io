Hey. Let's be quick, shall we? It's so early in the morning because of work and I think I might have thought too much of this girl for her to appear in my dreams just now so without further ado...




"I think I was at a certain festival or event or something, like those with an open setting. I walked by a line of booths on front stage to see a panel of line up products and their prices from left to right in decreasing prices.

"Wow they look pretty expensive" until I saw the lower end and it's 200 yen and I'm like "I can afford this"

And it's also because I saw who is manning the counter to sell the prize which is a bar of chocolate somehow: Takahashi Rie-san.

There wasn't a queue, I felt rather awkward, but I took out $2 from my wallet (note the differences in currency) and gave it to her. I thought getting even a bar of chocolate from Rieri would make my day. But as soon as I handed her the paper, she thanked me like she would thank any customer with a smile, kept the money away, and continued to serve the next customer.

And as I was wondering if something is off, I started to ponder if I read that panel wrong for it might be just to get a chance to get the prizes listed. Which means I did not win the prize. Of a bar of chocolate.

I walked away depressed while still enjoying the moment of happiness. I then thought to myself that if it's a game of chance I should try to win it. So I went to withdraw more money, but then gathered with my friends for a meal or something, while I still remembered to go spend this money and after a couple of hours later... The counters were closed. They were replaced by if I didn't remember wrongly, a special Earphones stage with the trio sitting on the panel, but something looks off when they introduced themselves: Kouno Marika looks surprisingly old in looks and sounds like a man.
