Mayoiga: Hmm... as an Anime original? Wow my expectations have dropped quite a lot now.
Okay. The story. It's just a bus of people how want to live another life outside society. And yes, I'm probably one of them if I'm dumb enough will believe it and register for such a tour. Obviously this may be a farce, the driver is so pissed they can all die at once, but then again for first episode this must be disappointing. Not much of the storyline to talk about, and then they didn't let anything important happen! I mean, because it's Anime original means they cannot show us the rumoured Nanaki Village like, RIGHT NOW?
I'll stay for another episode, probably. But for legit reasons I may not even bother to. The cast size is too large for anything to be consistent, and the uncertainty of the show is large. Even larger then Glasslip, who only has less than a dozen characters. I'll see about it. Likely not. Kudos to theme songs though. Not so much for the show as of now. This thing's going to be one cour (12-13 episodes)?
Bungou Stray Dogs: Wow. I'm stunned. I think the next ultimate combination of an action supernatural special human powers show is here. I'm hyped. ðŸ˜†
The story first. The introduction is captivating, as we're left with a story of not much of an origin. Here we are looking for a guy to survive after being kicked out of an orphanage and living without food for days... And hey, he decided to take action to survive. By stealing, but still, it's not a bad thing. In a strange turn of events, he saved a guy who is trying to kill himself (well, sort of; I'll explain later why) and ended up getting a free dinner. Or so he thought. He knows a lot about this mysterious tiger these guys were looking for, and surprisingly (well, at least, to me) it was Atsushi himself. The main guy. Well that would spell a good reason for this show to start even, considering any other excuse may be considered as lame in my opinion. Now, what's good about it. The comedy is mutual, I like it and I bet the author, with the help of the animation production company, has done a good job on it. If I can predict the next joke I'm going to like the show. (The reward if Atsushi helps to catch the "tiger" is unknown, but you get what he means, right? Me too.) So in that sense, I'll watch the show, right? Well, yes. It reminds me of remember my self-written story APoL? I think I can get a few ideas from there. Special powers in humans is kind of my thing, especially when little transformation is involved (okay besides that tiger thing), and undercover organisations are exciting, so why not? I'll watch a couple more episodes to see how this goes hehe
(P.S. The characters include an Edogawa Ranpo. Serious. Remember Ranpo Kitan from last year? Apparently this author hasn't received any copyright strikes yet haha. I wonder what his powers are...xD)
