Besides, we can always live with non-ideality, like ourselves.

Imperfection is always within. So it's no surprise if you don't fancy what others do. But even if it's far from expectations, don't give people the menacing look. Embrace it, even if it doesn't symbolise you. Of course that does not mean one should follow imperfection. But so long as you remember about it, you're fine being your own. No one in perfect.
