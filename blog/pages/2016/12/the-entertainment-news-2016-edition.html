ANIME
Bungou Stray Dogs: And... we're at the end of yet another fantastic season. Hey, this show is good, no matter how many times I say it.
In this last episode (24) we see the conclusion. Apparently it's just Francis who cannot hold on to his money for some reason (seriously reminds me about Anime "C" that I want to watch that again) and collapsed without a trace. And guess what, I was even more wrong as I continued watching. I thought Kyouka was to be the one who defeats Francis, and I really didn't know there was such an elaborate plot into planning for the Agency that even the President's ability came into play. And damn his ability is the best haha. On other things, and if the Anime isn't wrong, the manga is probably doing the arc with that demon Dazai mentioned, which I'll probably be reading since no one is teasing a new season of Anime for me. I'll read it though. But the epicness I may not be able to handle haha. All in all, a real good show that just got better with the wait of the second cour (as they are called in Japan), and I can't wait to see more of the action. Seriously, the only thing I've been missing. Not Atsushi's apologies or the smug faces of the Guild or whatever.

WWW.WORKING!!: And... we've reached the end of another season. Looks like they're not doing another one of these, huh. I'm going to miss the general series.
The last episode of this series (13) talks about concluding a lot of things, mainly between Higashida and Miyakoshi. The Saint Valentine conclusion her is rather expected not including the foretelling dream intrusions and stuff, but it seems Miyakoshi finally understood. And in return, Higashida also finally understood how to love. In many ways than other (I'm referring to his family here), that's good news to both. Second half officially concludes the series, with a changed Yuuta-kun, a flowerbed Shiho, a longer haired Miyakoshi, a shorter haired Nagata, a money-minded Hime-chan, and more. All in all, covering up all the romance ends that exist in this show. It may be a little regurgitation after a while, but all's well ends well. Again, damn I'm going to miss the laughs.
