Sorry people, in the end it is us, not religion, that is at fault.

We have created faith. We have protected it. And now we question it. Argh, such is human behaviour, I guess. Reading an article about the Pope (no I'm a free-thinker citing this for sake of discussion) talking about gender differences including transsexualism, and he is right. Who are we to judge? After all, we love to create problems for ourselves, and it's no responsibility for any higher being to guide us. Thus he guides. He's setting a good example for other religious leaders to follow, I must say.
