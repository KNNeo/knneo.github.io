Have I always been consciously fixing my minor mistakes? It takes repeating the mistake to understand how much good I've done to myself.

Really, I mean it. For example, I almost tripped whilst walking because I know that stepping on the edge of an elevated platform causes danger but I still did it. To my surprise, I did almost trip. Hey, didn't notice that coming, huh. We have been doing it. Preventing ourselves from danger.
