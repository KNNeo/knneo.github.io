If everybody were to talk about being kind, there wouldn't be talk about money.

Too bad, money conflicts out lives in so many ways, we even forgot that trading actually relies on being kind to each other in order to make and accept offers. Good thing there are still voluntary movements (not organisations) around to make us feel human. Same goes with working for a company. It's all for that paycheck eventually.
