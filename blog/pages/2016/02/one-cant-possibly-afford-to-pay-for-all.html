One can't possibly afford to pay for all entertainment today

Not because I'm a pirate of content or I only use free content, but then again it's quite true. If we have to pay for every performance and entertainment service today, the biggest problem would be the budget. It's difficult to feed the constant desire to find more enjoyable things to have. We can watch more paid movies, but we can only watch that much. Just like everything else in this world now.
