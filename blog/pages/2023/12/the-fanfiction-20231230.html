Ah, the final fanfic for the year.

Honestly saying, I did not expect to go this far with what I humbly started to celebrate this blog running for I don't know how many years, and Ten-chan as my phone wallpapers is really edging me to write more about the interactions between her and fictional me but heck, I gotta end this year with what I started, right? Which is a great excuse to put this girl again on the table? Man, I wish I can put her on the table if you know what I'm sayin'-

Anyhow, let's end this season like how fictional me would end it... and spend the new year with a woman I love?? I WILL NEVER lmao *dabs*




It's always alone for me at the end of the year since I moved into Tokyo, but not this year it won't be. Takahashi Rie, who I met on my new job? We have since started going out for the past few months, but it has always been because we both want to avoid the older co-worker crowd that make up our department who are still living in the Showa Era, that we chose to hang out together.

This time around though, I thought Takahashi invited me over to her place for New Year's to hang out with her friends or acquaintances, but as I entered her apartment and put down my gifts for the visit, there was no one else. I dared not take a step further into her living quarters which smelled better than a flowerbed, so I took a seat at a stool near the kitchen. I instinctively asked if they as a group have anything planned for this gathering. She let out a laugh, laid down prone onto a cyan-coloured sofa beside me, and looked back at me with a smile and a glint in her eyes.

"What do you want the two of us to do?"




Imagine that sounds exactly like Takagi-san though lol, but I don't know, Rieri. I would love to cuddle, maybe watch a movie together. *laughs* (Yeah, the movie was secondary haha) Oh well, guess I will never be with a woman and be alone forever, and all these will just be in writing or dreams if the ones this year aren't enough of an indicator of my sad, lonely life.

Moving on though, I will take a break for the new year. This series will come back, I don't have much confidence to do more now considering my mood swings just in the past few weeks, but I will be back. I want to write more of these just because (and we must not forget about Ten-chan lol), so I will come up with something. *thumbs up* See you then and have a happy new year! With Rieri though? I mean, only one man can be with her and it's Aozaki Shouhei- Or her dad, yeah, her dad back in her hometown haha *sigh*
