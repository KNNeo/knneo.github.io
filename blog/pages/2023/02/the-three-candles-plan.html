THE IDEA
So far in my pathetic life lol okay I will stop that we are trying to be positive here, I've only been living a life of coming back home from work and just binging on YouTube or even re-watching YouTube clips and/or watching Twitch archives every night because I just want to take breaks and not think too much after work. Therefore, the objective of this list of plans is to achieve all of not any the following:
  • To move away from "easy" and "addictive" entertainment, and not just watch YouTube all day

Which is why I had to spend some time to collect all the information I know, and list them here! Don't worry, the ones here are just for the purpose of this post, where I will also explain for a bit the terms and the podcasts themselves and what they are.

We will need some definitions right here, so I'll try to explain them all, keyword being try:
  • First half free - A concept to bait people into subscribing into their channels for a fee, eg. an hour podcast can be free for half an hour and paid for the other half, where of course the latter will have more relevant content and former be just reading fan mail and doing PR. The Seigura Niconico Channel is known for such a tactic.
  • Alternating weeks - As per definition, the podcast will be aired every 2 weeks, and the issue here is this can be categorized into two factions; Either referred to as "airing every other week" which is dependent on which week it originally started on (eg. aired last week, next will be next week) or "airing in certain weeks" which is now dependent on which week number of the month it airs on. (eg. Week 2 of February 2023 is considered to be on the week of Valentine's Day)
  • Simulcast vs Live - Each podcast has their own relevancy with respect to their broadcast timing. Some will choose to air at late night but not require their talents to do it live, so it will be pre-recorded and aired for the first time where people will tune in regularly known as a simulcast, similar to TV shows. Some will air live though, even with video content on special occasions or straight up as regular, nowadays taking it to YouTube for higher quality and audience reach compared to Niconico though with a more interactive comment system.
