THE ORIGIN
I guess I should start with when I knew of her. It's Anime, no surprises, Nagae Rika here is a Japanese voice actress after all. Most of us would have known her from 2018 Anime series "Asobi Asobase" voicing one of the 3 main girls as terrible-American Japanese Olivia haha, you may even know her from that isekai retirement Anime which is her new main role in a while, but actually, I already somehow knew of her a year before I really recognized her voice which was in 2017 Anime "PriPri Chii-chan" haha don't worry that animation is for kids, but she was voicing a male character! Yeah, surprising I didn't know of her back then, but yeah don't worry "Asobi Asobase" did make up for it. *laughs* From video specials I got to witness her lively personality in front of the camera (of course not as hype as 1x Kino Hina lol but this is not promoting her) and actually how adorable she can be, and probably even more. My memory is kinda fading over the years knowing of her as she often appears in the Anime I watch most recently "Aharen-san" and in online video series which she still is in one now "Tensai!? Rikachimalu", and podcasts like the one this publisher's parent company is featuring her in "Nagae Rika's 17 Changes"... she is trying her very best to appear in front of the Internet the best way she can. Trust me, even I did not believe she can get in the spotlight time and again, let alone release a photobook maybe for an occasion we don't know of because by the way, we do not know of her age. We will get to the disclaimer later hehe, but suffice it to say age doesn't stop us from liking her, not any lesser, and I think it's worthy to give her this book when it was announced end of last year. Anyways, Rikachimalu, this is months in the making, I really tried to avoid spoilers online apart from the publisher's official Twitter account just spoiling the heck out of me, but now that I got my hands on it let's go in blind to see what she has to offer!
