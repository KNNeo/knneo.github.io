So next up, this has been months in the making even years at this point I thank the staff for making this happen in the first place, we have some goods from Takahashi Minami and Oonishi Saori's video podcast series!! Wow where should I even start, probably 3 years ago when I started? I mean, I missed out on a lot of things from them including the initial episodes just because as we see them do this from their homes and them coming back into the studio (I think I started around here), from the many things they decided to do to create an "at-home" feel under the theme of a general store in Japan dressed up in jerseys (the old man in those typically wears... what old men wear lol), still gave us a lot of fun times from just an episode eating conveyor belt sushi (in the studio) to I think the funniest ones were when TakaMina had to answer elementary school riddles and she just can't. *laughs* They also surrounded themselves a lot around the community too. Their budget was based on the number of "Twitter" followers (lmao), they praised and pinned up fan artworks as the show's backdrop, and there were even plans of doing an IRL event this year to meet all the fans who supported them!

All of it was a great time, I wasn't the most dedicated of viewers to tune into this on Wednesday nights, but all this ended, if you forgot, because Oonishi here announced she will go on hiatus to recover from her "mental condition". Because of that, months ago where they started to substitute many guests in Oonishi's place (Hondo Kaede, Kitou Akari, to name a few) before she came back for one last hurrah so we already saw the signs, and they also cancelled the IRL event they were supposed to hold. More importantly, with that they also announced the final (pre-order) sale of all the stuff they have sold so far which included the sticker set (which is a first episode thing, they spoke in kindergarten voices to promote a made up kid-friendly theme park and thus created those "mascots") and teacup (which would've made this the first of its kind if I got it last year), along with new stuff like the clear folder and bromide photos set which *chef's kiss* is hard to come by for both of them tbh, so I genuinely will take this chance to thank everyone and for all of this from the bottom of my heart.
