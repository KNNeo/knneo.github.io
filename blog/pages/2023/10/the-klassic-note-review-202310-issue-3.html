音楽 The Klassic Note Review
Kitou Akari - Magie x Magie [Single]
First Press Limited EditionRegular EditionAnime Edition
(You see, if you want her to look back at you, you have to pay for the more expensive Limited Edition. Idolism in a nutshell, by the way. I'm just grateful for the close-ups.)

Origin: I think many of us do not know of her from her songs, but might have known of her through Anime. Being a voice actress from the days I knew of her voicing in romance stories I liked, to singing super cool songs to please, Kitou Akari has all the talent. And I think this is a long time coming just because, when we finally have an excuse to let her sing love songs through and through, she is once again singing for Anime since she started in 2019, but now putting her adorable image on the line. This time around, this is undoubtedly the love song single and do not let the title deceive you, and we shall talk about what makes this work special.

(1) Magie x Magie [Trans.: Unblinkingly]
From the first chorus:
"(I) wish (you) would like me back the same
Even if (you) are constantly hesitating
The (idea, that we) both like each other
(If) wish came true (doesn't) that count as love?"
I think my first impression of this song wasn't from the music video which is basically fanservice, so I can talk about what's good about it. It's a love song, for starters, and it's a catchy tune thanks to Arte Refact (this single is composed and produced by them too), and most important of all, thanks to the lyricist for coming up with lyrics to make us love Kitou Akari all over again. With the word "like" sprinkled all over the place it really feels like overkill, but with meaning, this suddenly becomes more than a song of flirting, as reflected by the Anime it is credited on where the main girl has a crush on the main guy who she has known for a long time. The bridge might be a little long, little imperfections here and there, but overall, she sung it cutely and I have been liking this song as much as it liked itself, I think it has delivered the message. The music video is basically the artist in person showing all her love on camera, and I cannot thank that enough.
Rating: Lovingly Above Average

(2) Afterglow
From the first verse:
"The reason (our) eye sights entwined (was) coincidence
Reason(s) other than that
Because thinking about (otherwise) is pointless
It is coincidence (and) that is fine"
As usual, the B-side is what makes of this single a whole, and I think this is easily one of the most complete feels I can give on a love song: the feels of sunset, the feelings of love growing uncertainly, and everything else in between. Clearly this song's lyrics talk about a maiden falling in love over time, and afraid to show her feelings. This cannot get better than that, I know this is rather typical to tackle on such a theme for a song, but the lyrics just makes it all the more complementary than it should have. The melody overall is sweet to listen to, it has a short bridge part which turns everything around even in terms of the lyrics which might have been excessive in those extra notes in the melody, but I just adore the general chill feels, so that's good enough for me.
Rating: Sweetly Average

Conclusion: If you are a fan of Kitou Akari, you will love this single as much as I do. If you aren't, I feel like this also captured the Anime's mood real well, the songs are decent to catch anyone's first impression of her which is definitely a good start, and I wished they did something like this sooner. This is that typical one-two punch of love songs in a single that will make me purchase this without hesitation: It's the underlying fuel that keeps the music sung by idols going.


- Aozaki Shouhei, on the Klassic Note
