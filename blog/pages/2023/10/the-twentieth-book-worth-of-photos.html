THE INTERVIEW
I feel like talking about her life so far especially her career and where she came from will be interesting, so for years since the last time I did this, I'm doing another interview summary!
(*Updated 2023.10.07)
  • On being asked about how she started being a voice actress, she started by saying that she wanted to be in musicals and studied it in university, and back then singing cover songs of Anime theme songs, by chance (I assume she got selected for auditions) she entered the world of entertainment. It was only afterwards where she expressed interest in "the world of 2.5D" and with roles at that time did she have a chance to challenge herself at this career.
  • Speaking of which, when she was acting for the Anime "K" series musical adaptation where her character was voice in the Anime by Horie Yui who was also the first artist she bought a CD of, but it was only because of the musical and knowing of the work was she aware of the term "voice actor".
  • On being asked why she intended to pursue the voice actor career despite being very different from an actress, she said that she has always been keen on the role of expression. Though the skills required for a stage actress is different from a voice actress, the point of "capturing one's character" and "fulfilling one's role" are still similar, so she thought in the long run, this will help her to expand her horizons on (that role of expression as an actor).
  • She knew that this was going to be a very drastic change, even so she still expressed her interest to be a voice actress to the talent company at that time (Office EN-JIN), that she really is keen on trying this out.
  • With respect to her first main role in Anime "Just Because!", it was when she has a year break from stage performance and was offered an audition opportunity, so she spent that time to study hard for this, and with all the roles she has starred in, she gave herself a 99 (out of 100) points rating, stating that there is still room for improvement despite putting in her best and supported by everyone in the past 6 years of voice acting.
  • She most definitely did not regret taking up this career, and as much as she didn't expect to be able to be able to reach this far (and she doesn't have much to do in her private time), she thought of having more work is already a joy. She thought of what she wanted to do otherwise, but still regarded voice acting as a lifework.
  • On being asked about her experience in her debut role in "Just Because!", she related to Lynn-san who was nice and honest with her, since she did not know much as a novice, and Lynn did taught her everything nicely like an elder sister, and was glad she had met her because of this, with nothing but filled with respect for. They did appear together again in works like "Uma Musume" series, but they also message each other on LINE privately. When she announced the release of this photobook, she also got contacted by Lynn soon after!
  • On being asked about the changes in her for the past decade living in her 20s, she said she went from being obedient and listening to what should be done, to being way stronger (in one's opinion) over time. It was also because she had only be in the "audience perspective" did it stop her from being better in expression of her acted roles. Today her is more about being her and letting it out, stating that with fans and give more opportunities to appear on camera for example appearing on variety shows, she is more than able to communicate with others more freely and openly.
  • Past her was also very blunt about things and being an overthinker, so in the context of Anime that makes her an otaku, which also makes her very biased in contrast, living by her own rules. 
  • This (along with her lack of communication skills) made her very far-reaching even for fans who noticed as she moved to talent agency AMUSE and was called "the royalty of AMUSE" which really made her rejective of the title, but learnt to accept it as part of her image, enjoying what the fans have bestowed upon her. With the years of acting though, she slowly learnt to be more independent towards her late 20s, and that has probably shown how far she has grown.
  • When asked about the ideal her, she talked about living life to the fullest and being oneself. As she gets older, she became more aware about the Isobe Karin that she wants to achieve.
  • As expected, she found out that if one doesn't like oneself it's hard to excel in work, to pull oneself up and work hard, and is what she often told to herself. For example, going into a (voice actor's) recording in the studio, one has to put confidence in their preparations without lying to oneself about their efforts, and put in their best. Because there's that fight against oneself to grasp the character voiced, singing the songs one knows the lyrics of, it's all about keeping the production of these works genuinely and carefully over time.
  • She treasures the people around her who are supportive of her, from her family, best friends to the staff and fans alike, specifically of a bestie she knew from elementary school where they still hang out from time to time, and even just send photos of an image from a book they read, where she thinks is more than just thankful for people who will think of her from their own lives.
  • She regards fans as much as she does to family and friends and even lovers, sort of along the lines of allies, which she says it is hard to describe. Seeing everyone pursue their dreams and watching her move along her career really makes her stronger, and at ease every day she comes home from work, which the interviewer describes as "being at home", with her specifically talking about being a futon being a type of charger to continue to progress, and laughs.
  • On the announcement of creating of this photobook, she has always voiced out to her manager that she wanted to do such a thing, fans have been raising about such a prospect for years, and am honestly glad she is able to do this now.
  • It also fulfilled her dream of going to Okinawa for the first time, seeing such a beautiful place makes her want to visit again.
  • She likes Okinawa only how different it is from her hometown (Nara Prefecture), as there are many beautiful sceneries and broad skies (likely unobstructed horizons) and has that overseas island feel that she liked.
  • She felt like if she did this photobook a few years earlier, she would not have been able to do these expressions as well as she can now. Since it's her first time trying out close-up shots and being fashionable or being matured, instead of the usual "cute" image. She thinks that this was a big change in accepting these positively, which was described as "the me now", and focus on gathering such feels was the theme of this photobook.
  • On asked about what she wants to deliver to her fans with this, she said that this is to let fans set further expectations of her down the road, and use this as proof to believe in the Isobe Karin they know and love, also specifically to fans who have voiced out about wanting this for a long time, this was her thanks and response to those.
