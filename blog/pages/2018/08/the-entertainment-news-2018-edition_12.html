"Looks like she's really into a band she saw at Sakuragaoka High School" lmao oh damn the feels. Okay, in case you didn't know, "K-ON!" was a legend. It broke out as an Anime sensation back in 2009 of what might be the standard for many music-based shows to follow. But now, the original author Kakifly had a different idea. So in comes the next work, totally original, not based on anything before (well, subtle hints indicate they might be), with a different cast and setting, but the premise is the same: It's some girl who liked how cool band music is and decide to start a journey of her own. Before this series it's Tainaka Ritsu if you need a reminder (who dragged her friend Mio to quit her application for Literature Club I think, after reminiscing themselves a flashback of watching an awesome live performance), and this time, it's Sakuma Yukari, a spiritual successor to Ricchan but behaves like a 5-year-old. Basically, Ricchan if she is Yui-chan lol. Her partner, Shimizu Kaede, seems to be an easy-going person, going along with the friend since middle school unlike the one bassist we're familiar of. Clamped in with (literally) Maho-chan (family name unknown?) who is just an innocent classmate who is currently as of now, here to fill in the blanks for the light music (because they don't have clubs of this type, and) appreciation society who's main purpose does not contribute and aren't recognised by the school, contains only one senior. Satou Riko-chan here, seems to be enjoying her studying in this to-be-evicted location since she decorated it really well. Of course, drum kit included so Yukari-chan decided to have a go. That's pretty much it though for the first chapter (1) it would seem, so let's talk more about what we have seen. Clearly Kakifly-sensei wants to convey the point of being impressed by music to performing music once again, and in essence I think it has been fulfilled. The similarities between the series stop there though, as now we have a senior in our hands instead of a junior (Azunyan if you need a reminder), and... they technically aren't recognised by the school. Although this might just be a good thing since it'll take some time before they get together everything to be even believed that this club exists (it's November apparently), and they technically still need an advisor. Well, I assume they have a teacher behind this, but yeah, it'll take some explaining. I conclude this with a statement to remind you all that this is NOT the original work: bear in mind there aren't going to be any old stuff to remind you of (like Mio-chan's embarrassment, Yui-chan's stupidity) (okay maybe the latter) so if they don't deliver don't be disappointed. But be promised of something, that the girls will still be heading for the road to success as they attempt to form the next music band in Anime history.
