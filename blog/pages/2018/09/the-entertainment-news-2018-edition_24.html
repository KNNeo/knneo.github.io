In this new episode (#Tsukumogami10) of this rental shop series we finally get into #TheGossip of the most important roles of them all: how Izumoya came to be. And it seems like they have a backer. It's no Kickstarter by all means, but hey Seiji likes money can't you see it in his face? *laughs* Okay serious talk, this episode is about selling stuff to people. Specifically, Usagi the comb apparently. Why? It's not that important at this point because we know that it will never happen no matter what, but there are a few important things to take note. Firstly, the shop is in debt. 20 ryo if you need a number. At first it was a lending just like how this shop works, but sooner or later with interest (Hey you thought it didn't exist in the Edo period?) this adds up to a lot, coinciding to the point of two clients asking to buy their lending-only goods. I mean, we wouldn't be happy if they sold Usagi off to be honest, and I'm still glad they didn't because I legit teared up *sob*, but it's more than just this. Secondly, we know very little about Okou's story. Remember that Suou thing that Sataro left with? Not true, ladies and gentlemen, for he told the only one he had a crush on and trusted, which is Okou. So obviously it didn't get stolen, and if it did, it was with the fire. So how did the rumour go away with the man? Someone forked out 80 ryo to buy him another incense burner to save his reputation and a potential marriage, you see. Very high price for keeping someone alive potentially, but he left too anyway. Well, at least the money wasn't a problem to get him hunted down and killed. Ignoring his fading trace, Okou seems real concerned about sharing this with someone, so she took a tsukumogami (or two) and told them. I don't think we ever got to know more of that thereafter because I don't think Notetsu and Usagi will tell the others, really. Lastly, that priest standing outside their store.
