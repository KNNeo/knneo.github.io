アニメ Alice to Zouroku
Wow what an ending. I guess all's well ends well in this fantastic show. I mean, it's magic after all haha
The final episode (#AliceToZouroku12) concludes on the two lost in Wonderland (literally). They surface, managed to find that rabbit to contact the outside world, and guess who came to save them. I know, Ayumu was literally cheating right here haha. Then again, the world shows all sorts of insights into an existence of an alternate world, as narrated by the ending of this series. And finally, Naito has decided to allow the government (specifically the police) to announce on Dreams of Alice so that more people can be saved, other than Hatori, to not feel segregated with human society. But of course, Sana-chan probably got it worst because she started with the least exposure to the human world (I mean, the twins aren't even born from Wonderland) because she was created there, and thus is the likely "true embodiment" of Alice, as mentioned. So yeah, Sana-chan may just have seen a figment of her future. Remember that cool woman? All in all, an interesting series and quite an insight to a less conflicting, more consoling depiction of the existence of such a magical world. But hey, I'm even more glad to have watched it. Already soaked in "tearbath" by the time Hatori went home to see her parents. So... if you haven't been watching this show I tell you it's arguably the best new show of the season (ie. excluding sequels), and this really makes me want to know more. Maybe I should read the manga then.

アニメ Tsuki ga Kirei
Okay not to spoil you guys on anything, but Koutarou got in to a public school. With Chinatsu in it. Oops.
The final episode (#TsukiGaKirei12) concludes on this story (because I just noticed this is an original Anime lol) about the couple, how Akanecch is still stuck with emotions of jealousy and doubting her partner and Koutarou being... indirect as usual, in conveying his feeling to his partner. All in all, what has happened in the whole of this series. Let us be reminded that, despite this being the reality of their relationship, they still managed to pull it off time after time, through coincidences (thanks, God-sama) and everyone around them. I must say, tears will not be able to express how much I liked this series. (I'll never use the word "love" as I have never, so forgive me) That first love feeling and being together is portrayed really well, and it's not like I have any experience to prove it, but seeing as this is also popular unto itself, I expect so. Man, I can't wait to have my own experience. I mean, I failed at the confession part already, so... I guess sincerity is important. And reporting to the wife about things around you too, it seems. Oops, I said too much. I'll leave the show in your hands if you still are watching, because there really isn't another Anime I'll watch that will capture the feel of this show. First time romance, it's awesome. Pity that I'll not be experiencing that again in the future. (I mean, the "miracle" of someone liking you back didn't happen anyway)
