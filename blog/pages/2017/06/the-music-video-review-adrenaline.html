The Music Video Review - adrenaline!!!
Hey, it's me again. I thought I'll never have to do another music video review (the last time was in 2015) but I couldn't resist. After looking at the awesome Anime theme song of the season (arguably) I have to do it. Backed up by Amamiya Sora's blog post about music video timestamps featuring not her lol, I decided to give Ten-chan some credit by putting it into a full post instead of talking about it in Entertainment News. Then again, the previous one just passed, so this is just me being desperate haha.

Let's begin, shall we?

Here I shall talk in two perspectives: Ten-chan on shots of Asakura Momo (Mocho) and Natsukawa Shiina (Nansu), and then me, of shots of her. Of course, I'll keep this as unbiased as possible, even though any bias might just truly represent who I like more hehe


THE PLOT
I mean, is there? This music video generally fits in the theme of TrySail, ie. sailing to greater heights if that's what the production wants. They immerse themselves in scenes of exploration and being in the seas. Anyway, I think this brings up a notch of what they did in previous MVs, as they play around with a couple of scenes, totally enjoying themselves. And to say that only may also be an understatement. You can see it for yourself. I saw it myself and I'm impressed. At the fanservice haha
