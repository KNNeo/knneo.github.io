I want to be impressed. And in order to do that, I have to set aside my own experience.

After not being able to unsee harsh comments online doing nothing but criticising media really breaks my heart, especially if you're impressed with it. Oh well. What does it mean to be experienced when you want more, criticise everything that is not what you expect, and never give a chance for perspective? Growing up somehow makes some of us very narrow in comparison.
