The Dream Fragment 20190901 (And Someone Else's Dream Record)
Well Nan-chan decided to tell us about her dream in her blog so I decided to tell mine too. 100% because of her, by the way haha

I'll put a snippet as to what she said about her dream, but first let's talk about mine. Ready? Yes, it's about the TrySail girls, just a heads up.




"I was in a nearby mall (well Tampines Mall isn't exactly nearby so...) (don't you dare Google on it) in which I remembered (of course, it's not real) that the TrySail girls were handing out bromides ie. commemorative photos I assume promoting something nearby so of course I had to go get some for myself. Firstly, Nan-chan. It looked like she was hesitantly giving them out, but I went forward and snatched it like no one's business. At least she was happy seeing it being taken. (It was as if it was pathetic that no one knows them in the land of Singapore) (not true I'm not the only fan here I promise just look at the AFA they were in) Ten-chan was next. I took the bunch of them with the smile on her face somewhat making sure I will take it no matter what it is, and sorry to Mocho just because you were last. The weird thing about the dream though, that (1) it didn't stop me from getting more so by the end of it my hands were full and they were all different, and (2) there seemed to be multiple of them, likely handing different items out at different timing maybe. It felt like those people giving out flyers who bypassers won't give too much care about, but at least I'm glad I am the one who patronised."




Well, considering I woke up quite a while and it was still quite vivid and I only can remember this much, I guess it wasn't so much of a dream as much of an expectation if I were in Japan or whatever haha. Also, my imouto's impression is the best which also geared me to write this (and below later) too anyways. Cya guys around then.
