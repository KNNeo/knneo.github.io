"Wait, what?"

You may wonder to yourself what is with the title and the opening I chose above for this post, because besides the reasons behind why I wanted to write this celebratory birthday post of what I got for myself, it's also to ask myself what I think of these girls that I've truly, for this case, seen them grow from the beginning. Well, at least, the closest to the beginning I know of.


THE BACKGROUND
A little bit of background before we start so that you can feel how hyped I am already at this point. (Since after this it's most likely just images lol) When it was close to my birthday many things happened, and among all of them one Natsukawa Shiina announced her first original photobook (unlike her real first which was from a compiled series in a magazine) which will be releasing exactly one day after my birthday. Of course, it will not be able to reach my hands immediately considering I don't live in Japan, so this had to wait. And suddenly, a thought came into my mind, and I said to myself "Why not give all three of them?" and yes, I meant the TrySail girls. Those girls I gave like half a dozen CDs on if you knew of Klassic Note. So I went on a journey to acquire all three photobooks in very different ways since the other two from Asakura Momo and Amamiya Sora aren't that new as of writing (about six months after release already), so... yeah. I decided to talk about what I got now. At least, on my birthday, I did not regret the least of clicking the order button on all three photobooks, now that I got to see them all. *salivates and kisses books*

THE HAPPENINGS
Okay ONE more paragraph before we start (haha I know you guys anxious to see boobs lmao) (only cleavage though, I can promise only that) because I have to talk about how far I went to get these. For Natsukawa Shiina (Nan-chan) it had to be a pre-order (YES!) and there was a new proxy shipping service AOCS in town, so I decided to try it out. Eh, mostly because of free handling fees for first time but I swear it wasn't much and Tenso is faster haha. I'm glad it got here today and not next week though, thank goodness. For Asakura Momo (Mocho) and Amamiya Sora (Ten-chan) I got their books through Rakuten and then Buyee, which probably costed a lot more if not considering I wanted to buy everything else in one place. (we'll get to that later, but) Then again it was cheaper considering how long they've been around, so heck, I'll take that. I also considered to complete the collection like Nan-chan's photobook with a special gift from the pre-order, and since I didn't pre-order for the other, I decided to (yes, illegally) snatch a couple online where I can. Mocho's special gift came from an auction (we'll talk about the condition of the goods later) and it was like 5 bucks (excluding shipping and handling fees lol) so yeah why not? Ten-chan's special gift was from a shop I think, and it's also cheap too (considering I haven't paid for shipping lol) so yeah, that was one of the few choices I had to make. All three gifts were B2 posters though, as I'll show you guys later. Obviously the not pre-order goods came in faster, so it had to sit around for the week which was a week after my birthday (you know how hard it is to not open inside to see the books when it's THIS close to you?) and thank goodness the pre-order came in as expected since my country's postal service works Saturdays and I don't have to review these next Monday. Phew! Okay, let's get into it, shall we? *wipes nosebleed*
