This Week on Klassic Note
Hmm not much for this week, if not for last week. So I had to do reviews for last week too, including sphere’s new album and NANO-MUGEN COMPILATION. Over the week have been fascinated by the songs I hear from the Ajikan compilation, mostly the first few, and listened to people like NICO Touches The Walls (recall three years ago “Matoryoshika”) and Good Morning America, which sounds and looks like SPYAIR in many ways (someone who loves to wear short pants is the bassist) than other.
