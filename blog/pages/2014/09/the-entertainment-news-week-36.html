This Week on Klassic Note
Well since I forgot last week, then I shall mention it this week. Besides it’s the week where I review Hikasa Youko’s first album since her debut last year. Yocchan, as I (or many) would call her, did pretty well for this one. Though not perfect, and plenty of songs that are more lively than her and actually testing her high pitch singing, I see only a few chances for her in my list. Nevertheless I took down three songs (including the topic song) to support her, though definitely not enough for me to get a copy of her album. Too bad. Next time, yes? On the other hand, I’m still waiting for other releases like Oreskaband’s new mini-album “Carry On” for next week.
On new song updates, Ayachi’s new single has a song preview. Also her first single with an Anime tie-up (Denkigai no Honya-san, I mentioned this before weeks ago), she seems to be doing well, with her cute voice supposedly suitable for the show. Wondering what kind of show I’m expecting over here. Also, SPECIAL OTHERS ACOUSTIC new slbum has got a new PV preview online, and it makes me excited. Really looking forward to the album next month already.


That’s all I guess. I want to go back to playing in the last few hours of the week available to me before going back to my main job. Cya guys then, until next time… Mikakoshi’s selfie is going to stay on my phone wallpaper. Really, it’s been half a week already…
