ANIME
Sore ga Seiyuu!: This must be their best episode yet. Following Ichigo-chan's injury, and after a long time talking with Hocchan (yeah Alice5 is like, out for about 8 years and she can say she's in a group) made Futaba realise that she really treasures the group. Of course, as a professional. And that makes sense, from a industry perspective. I can recall just weeks ago where sphere (the only idol group I'm into at the moment) has a live niconico broadcast promoting their latest single (yes the July one) and Tomacchan is absent. So yeah, for the good of the group, it's better of if you get rid of them. LOL Anyways, I must say this is good, I thought we never get to hear a new song again (this one is good, I must say), and if you did notice Ichigo's father is present. This show definitely helps to give us a quirky twist of reality in a part of the entertainment industry in Japan. Really, producers don't go crazy if you tell them you broke a leg; they'll be smarter than Futaba to tweak the event to work in the artists' favour anyway.
