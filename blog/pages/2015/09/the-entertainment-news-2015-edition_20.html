ANIME
Sore ga Seiyuu!: Another episode of nothing but voice acting, well almost. Futaba was down from work, and not as bad as this episode's guest Shiraishi Ryouko described. Their first live concert is near, and it's still pretty empty and now Ichigo-chan has sprained her ankle. Oh well. Looks like they're going to end this season with a live performance. Hopefully there'll be enough people to fill up the place. Will Ichigo-chan be able to recover from her injury? We don't know. It'll be interesting to see how they end this show. (Funny that not all the stuff from the manga is mentioned in this show, but oh well what the heck)
Ranpo Kitan: Yes we're at the last episode, and seriously, it's worth thinking. Of course not many would watch this, or we'll have plenty of sympathisers all around the Twenty Faces criminal, but then again doing evil in the name of justice against societal norm is not an everyday topic in Anime. Nonetheless, I think this is worth thinking about, and you bet I've been thinking about it. Supposedly it's known that both Kobayashi and Namikoshi can be saved, but then the latter doesn't have much of a will to live, so yeah, the continuation of whatever formula he's talking about as a creator will still live on. Fact is, and if you don't feel like one of them because you lead a good life or something that you can be happy in, than let me tell you: It's not real. Doing up something like that as a formula is very impractical (if you Google the word "Laplace" you'll know what I mean) in society, and the worst case scenario is as of the terrorist situation of today, that you will be stopped and most of us will not give a damn about it. Besides, it's your goddamn life. If you want to throw it away instead of thinking how to break through that which you don't like, don't call yourself weak and force it upon the people who have harmed you. Period. I better stop here. Any more and my mask will slip, lol. Anyways, nice show, and didn't regret picking it up halfway through the show. Takahashi Rie too, thanks to you this was a nice show to pick up. *Kobayashi voice* Yeah!
WORKING!!!: Wow, that's plenty of development to absorb for this episode. In the latest episode, we see a final conclusion to start on Takanashi's feelings on Inami-chan (the mother's reaction at the end really is freaking me out), and the biggest thing that is going to happen in the restaurant, which is Yachiyo stopping her work in Wagnaria. Hmm... I won't say that is bad, but then now that Popura-chan is joining in the fun with her own problem, can't wait to see how they're going to conclude this one. I may not have read the manga, but if this is what the story shall end with it might just be the most interesting thing I'm expecting ever since I watched this like, 5 years ago.
