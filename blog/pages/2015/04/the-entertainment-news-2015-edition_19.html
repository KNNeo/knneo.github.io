Punch Line: Another episode of… not panties apparently, but more on the secrets of the tenants, which bring a lot of surprises as well as cliffhangers. Ansatsu Kyoushitsu: This episode brings a new PE teacher to replace the professional Karasuma-sensei, and he faces some competition, and also reality. Using fear to dominate the students like one would teach in the military does not work on Class 3-E, so he’s screwed (by the Principal). On the other hand, Karasuma does have his respect for students as assassins, so I’m giving him the salute for that. Koro-sensei is… totally useless in this episode. LOL
Plastic Memories: Tsukasa apparently gets to stay with Isla, her Giftia partner, which brings jealousy all over the cast (“Operation Lucky Pervert”, yes?), but also reveals more about her closed personality, the robot. That she actually doesn’t want to have further social interactions, if any, and when not necessary. That is why this may be the cause, for her knowing her time is up and that she doesn’t want to “create memories” with others in the know that it’ll end for her anyway. But that is wrong Isla-chan; people love that memory, even if it will disappear.
I think I’m done with this show now. Haha jkjk. Which also means I’m okay with how the ending will (prospectively) look like, and I’m watching it. And I think I can give the opening theme song. Not so much for the other theme song though. I shall see about it.
