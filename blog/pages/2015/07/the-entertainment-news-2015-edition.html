ANIME
Sore ga Seiyuu!: The trio is awesome, in some way or another. Or just the industry is trying to create another group of voice actors. Intentions aside, this episode is pretty expected, as they screw up their first radio live broadcast with guest Tamura Yukari (pls don't be deceived by her appearance in the show AND in real life) (srsly her twinkling stars are getting on my nerves) with nothing but free talk, and their own where they don't know what to say. There's also an emotional moment too, to remember Pipo for the last time lol. Honestly saying, the voices are really trained well even if it's empty as a radio show, and things like this probably won't happen (don't know about that slack director though) but many a times they are prepared and reminded, especially when you really have not much to say. A recent good example will be some Radio Gree Night with Kana-chan and Ten-chan, where in the first episode there still is reason to talk about how they met and their idea of such a show. Pity they are not taught to say that. Well at least we know now.
WORKING!!!: Wow another episode... man I thought this show is going to become a romantic comedy Anime already, serious. Anyways, Satou and Yachiyo got off their awkwardness last episode (And KitaEri acting confused and all in her natural voice is AWESOME! Hmph!), and we focus back to people around them, mainly people who want Satou dead (see quiz master and baseball bat for details lol), and other romantic antics around the restaurant. Looks like Takanashi's about to change his perspective in determining cuteness (apparently it's all about chests, really), as we all really want to see this couple come to some conclusion. Come on, don't we? After they've gone through so much? After Takanashi cross-dressed as a female? I'm looking forward to every episode of this show recently. Which is great, haha.
