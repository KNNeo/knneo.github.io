The Music Video Review - DREAMS, Count down!
I think you know why we're here for. We're here to watch the seriously awesome time we get to see the sphere girls dress up as OLs and dance the hell out of it in the office, things that will never happen in any other office in this world besides that studio. Anyways, I shall say pictures speak better than words (actually, "images"), so let's get to it.

In summary, this MV talks a lot about dancing, and they have promoted it on YouTube in order to encourage fans to learn this dance to do it with them during their live performances (which I did not learn, and did not go), and of course besides that there's more. Suffice it to say there are two versions of the video, probably one which is the original dance version named as "STOMP DANCE" and the other which we'll show a lot more of and where most of the images are from, named as "STAGE DANCE" version. From my knowledge there isn't a normal version music video and these two found in the limited edition DVDs are considered as "Additional music video", so I don't know why. But then what's interesting in the 2nd additional version is that there is drama. And pretty serious stuff linking things in the office with the fetish for rhythm and dance. Let's get to it!
