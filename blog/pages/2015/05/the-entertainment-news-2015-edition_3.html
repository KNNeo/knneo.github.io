アニメ
ANIME
Punch Line: More service possibly, but then a lot more story development. People step in to Rabura’s life to court her or even mess up her life’s interests and favourite food, but then more than ever are people reminding her of the things around her. Like wanting to find the hacker in the house to solve the world’s problem now or even save someone from being killed. Yeah, that cliffhanger of Ito-chan on the ground is… is just too much. Of course it may be a hoax, but then I see more than that compared to… shows I don’t even want to mention again. I shall see what happens, and take it in when it happens. Many secrets to an original Anime, so I guess…
Ansatsu Kyoushitsu: The exams are near, which also means the story is ending soon (remember the end of the world thing? yeah hopefully you didn’t forget because of Koro-sensei), and which also means more competition between the elites and the… potential elites supposedly to be idiots are taking it to the floor. Oh well this wouldn’t go well anyway, but I’m more interested in the ending. For this season, at least we know it’s not going to be complete, but I’ll take a happy ending, if possible.
Plastic Memories: I’m getting used to the theme songs already, I must admit. Sadly the songs only come another month after this, so… oh well. Anyways, about the story. It’s gotten serious. Obviously the fact that things must get worse one way or another as hinted from the last episode, this episode brings things to a new level because they left a cliffhanger of Isla potentially… being wiped off her functions. Oops. It may be Tsukasa’s fault for all we know (we also know that she revealed her intentions as a Giftia to him too), but then this may be a good chance to be friends with her again. Yeah, but this friendship may be a short-lived one: Isla has less than 1000 hours left anyway. Any way this goes it’s going to be great, so I’ll be looking forward to that.
