アニメ
ANIME
Punch Line: Hmm how should I say it… This show feels weird. Don’t mind me, the animation is great (Ito-chan crying so much I want to hug Mina-chan) (?) and the suspense is there, the story still feels believable (spirit’s ability to reverse back time to save someone), but NOT ANOTHER CLIFFHANGER?? Seriously man, this is getting boring. Funny thing is Meika is some robot, so we do know she may just survive this attack. Unfortunately the ending just gives one the cringing feeling that I have to watch the next episode. Seriously, man. Anyways, the end is near, like the end of the year in the story is near, and something must happen. We’re close to halfway through the show, and I can’t wait so see more plot. Not cliffhangers, please. I had enough of those…
Ansatsu Kyoushitsu Class 3-AB: (Like, just between Classes A and B) Seriously, they can claim that title. The last class owned three of five main subjects in the school, and that is only mid-terms. That maybe the good news, but that also means we’re not over. Remember season 2? (Audience: OMG…) Hell yeah. All content next episode onwards may just be filler, so I guess that’s a problem. I’ll still watch though; the comedy will win over everything anyway.
Plastic Memories: Conversely to Punch Line, this actually has some great story-telling going on. Recalling back to Isla’s past so that they can tell you that she didn’t get hit by the ultimate weapon last episode may be a bitch, but at least she’s fine. What’s not fine is her time is going to be up. What’s fine is Tsukasa doesn’t mind being with her till her end. Which is great, seeing that scene from Episode 1, yes? I’d like to see this come to an end, but I know the production will make a happy ending. I just hope they don't. They can always replace Isla to conclude the season and create another season? Hehe, I don’t know about that…
