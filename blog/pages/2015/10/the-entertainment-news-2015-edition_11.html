I may be wrong, but hey, I can watch a couple of episodes before making a decision. Honestly saying, this show will not catch my attention if it continues to make elaborate plot (dialogues aren't fluid probably in Sakurako-san's accent, and narratives are aplenty), then besides the introduction to this show I guess this may not be worth watching. Very likely I'll still drop this. I shall see about it for myself. Mysteries must be mysterious in nature, you know.
